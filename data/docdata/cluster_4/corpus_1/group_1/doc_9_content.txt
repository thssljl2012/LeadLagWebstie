 Paul Ince suffered his first defeat as a Barclays Premier League manager as his Blackburn side succumbed 4-1 to the club where he began his career in the game.Blackburn missed a penalty and failed to claw back an early two-goal deficit and were finished off by substitute Craig Bellamy and Carlton Cole in stoppage-time.West Ham defender Calum Davenport had headed in Julien Faubert's corner to give the home side the lead after 12 minutes, before Chris Samba's own goal doubled their lead. Jason Roberts missed a spot-kick before pulling a goal back, but Bellamy and Ashton struck in the closing stages to finish off the visitors.
