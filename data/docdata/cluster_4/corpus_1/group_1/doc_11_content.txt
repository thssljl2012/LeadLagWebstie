 ASB says some New Zealanders have been stung in a credit card scam while visiting the United KingdomThousands of New Zealanders who have travelled to the UK may have been caught up in an elaborate credit card scam.A credit card counterfeit ring has been operating in the UK since March.ASB Bank's Rob Campbell says some New Zealanders who have used their Visa or MasterCard while in the UK have been stung by the fraudsters and the bank has cancelled their cards.He says ASB has been monitoring accounts very carefully and assisting customers with replacement cards to ensure their travel is disrupted as little as possible.Mr Campbell is warning people not to let their credit cards out of their sight and to monitor their statements.
