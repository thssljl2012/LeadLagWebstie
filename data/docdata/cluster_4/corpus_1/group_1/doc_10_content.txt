 West Brom have completed the 2million signing of defender Abdoulaye Meite from Bolton.The Ivory Coast international passed a medical on Sunday afternoon before agreeing a three-year contract with the Barclays Premier League new boys.The fee for the 27-year-old could rise to 2.5million with performance-related add-ons, while the Baggies also have the option to retain his services for a fourth year.
