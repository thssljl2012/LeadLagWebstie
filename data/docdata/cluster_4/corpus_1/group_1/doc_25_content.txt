 One of football's most prestigious trophies is coming to town.The Premier League trophy will be at Barclays on Balfour Court Monday for football fans to see as part of a whirlwind UK tour.Visitors will have the opportunity to have their picture taken with the trophy when it visits the Barclays branch. The branch will be decorated with a football theme.Fans wishing to have their picture taken with the trophy can make a donation to Cash for Kids and Barclays will match the cash.
