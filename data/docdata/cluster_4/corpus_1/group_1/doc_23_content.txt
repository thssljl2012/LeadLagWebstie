 A bank customer was stabbed in the stomach when he tried to foil an attempted armed robbery in Merseyside.The 53-year-old victim was in the Lloyds TSB branch in Rock Ferry, Wirral, when a man walked in and threatened a security guard making a cash delivery. The offender, who was carrying a knife, knocked the guard to the floor and then stabbed the customer when he intervened, police said.The customer, from Bebington, was taken to Arrowe Park Hospital following the incident at about 1.15pm on Tuesday.His condition is described as stable while the security guard received a superficial hand wound.
