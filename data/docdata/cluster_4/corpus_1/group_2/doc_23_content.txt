The robbery took place in Dawley's High Street on 5 SeptemberDetectives investigating a bank raid in Shropshire are searching through a month of CCTV footage looking for clues to lead them to the robbers.Three men wearing balaclavas broke into Lloyds TSB bank in High Street, Dawley, Telford, on 5 September. They drove away in a black VW Golf car, later found in Little Dawley. Officers said they hoped to find clues, even from days before the robbery, which could lead to the identification of the gang.
