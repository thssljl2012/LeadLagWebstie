First to go was Northern Rock, at Newcastle United. Then West Ham had to ditch crashed tour operator XL. Worldwide insurance giant AIG, sponsors of Manchester United, was nationalised by the US government. Now Bradford Citys sponsor Bradford and Bingley has been bailed out. JJB Sports (Wigan), Britannia Building Society (Stoke), KCom Group (Hull), Garmin (Middlesbrough), Waitrose (Reading) and Cheshire Building Society (Macclesfield) are also suffering.
