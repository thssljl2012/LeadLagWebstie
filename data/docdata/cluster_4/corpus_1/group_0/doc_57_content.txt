 Beginning next month, Ghanaian football fans will be treated to a festival of the beautiful game as the English Barclays Premier League kicks off with Michael Essiens Chelsea, Emmanuel Adebayors Arsenal and Chelsea battling in a bid to dethrone Sir Alex Fergusons Manchester United.Gateway Broadcasting Services, which has exclusive rights in sub-Saharan Africa, will bring Ghanaians closer to their stars like Essien, Portsmouths Sulley Muntari, John Paintsil and other big name stars when the worlds most watched football league kicks off on August 16, with Manchester United and Newcastle United clashing at Old Trafford.
