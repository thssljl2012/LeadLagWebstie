 The ANZ Bank will replace Bill Express terminals at newsagencies after the collapse of the bill payment company.Bill Express went into administration yesterday, owing more than $180 million.The situation has left newsagencies unable to process bill payments, electronic mobile phone recharge pins, and the Bill Express-owned Bopo pre-paid credit cards.The ANZ was in partnership with the Melbourne-based company.Its spokeswoman Cherelle Murphy says more than 2 thousand Eftpos machines will be sent to the affected outlets. 'The ANZ terminals will allow the merchant customers to continue to accept credit card payments these should arrive mostly today,' she said.
