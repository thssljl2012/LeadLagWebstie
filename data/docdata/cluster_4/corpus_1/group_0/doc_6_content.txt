 Wigan have completed the signing of Egypt international striker Amr Zaki.The 25-year-old Zamalek striker has passed a medical and has put pen to paper on a one-year loan deal with the Barclays Premier League club. 'I've come to England to score goals. That's what I do,' Zaki said. 'I want to show the manager and the fans of Wigan that they have made a good choice and now I am totally focused on trying to get in the squad and prove myself here.'
