 The 1-million-square-foot Bank of America Building in Downtown Concord is for sale, and for only 200-million dollars!The property includes 4 buildings and 15 acres, and currently holds the bank's West Coast data center.Over 2,500 employees work at the site near the Concord BART station. Once sold, according to the East Bay Business Times, Bank of America is seeking to lease the property back from the new owner for at least two years in order to make better use of the unused equity, which is reinvested back into the business.Thanks to "Monument Residente" for the information on the story!
