Aston Villa boss Martin O'Neill is preparing a &amp;pound;10 million bid for Celtic winger Aiden McGeady.  The Daily Mail says O'Neill has been alerted that the Republic of Ireland star wants to make a move to the Barclays Premier League and is keen to make a bid.  McGeady, 22, is enjoying playing in the Champions League but is desperate to try his luck in England. He is also wanted by Sunderland boss Roy Keane, while north-east rivals Newcastle were also keen.
