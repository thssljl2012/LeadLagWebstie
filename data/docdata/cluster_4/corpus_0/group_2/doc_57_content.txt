Police in Shrewsbury are appealing for witnesses to an assault in which a man suffered cuts to his face. The incident happened near Lloyds TSB bank in Mount Pleasant Road between about 11.20pm and 11.30pm on Saturday(SEP 6). The victim was attacked by one of two males. Police attended but the offender was not found. The victim was treated by paramedics for his injuries, which are not believed to have been serious. Anyone who witnessed the attack is asked to contact Shrewsbury Police Station on 08457 444888 or the anonymous Crimestoppers number 0800 555111.
