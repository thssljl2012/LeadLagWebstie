Tottenham boss Juande Ramos admitted some relief after last night's 2-1 UEFA Cup first-leg victory over Wisla Krakow.  Darren Bent headed a winner 17 minutes from full-time to earn a 2-1 lead to take to Poland in a fortnight, with Tomas Jirsak cancelling out David Bentley's opener in the first half.
