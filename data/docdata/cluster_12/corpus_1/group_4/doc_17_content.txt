Article from: AAP  Font size: Decrease Increase  : Email  : Print  ANZ says it has a credit exposure to childcare provider ABC Learning Centres of about $182 million.  ANZ made the disclosure after ABC Learning went into administration today and its bankers subsequently appointed receivers and managers.  'ABC Learning Centres group was treated as a non-performing loan and an individual provision taken as part of ANZ's 2008 results,' the bank said.  'ANZ will make further adjustments to provisions as required.'  ANZ also confirmed its exposure to Allco Finance Group was insignificant.
