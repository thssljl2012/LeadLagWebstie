The developer of the Dicks Sporting Goods headquarters project in Findlay Township has secured $107.5 million in financing for the $150 million, 730,000-square-foot complex.  Horizon Properties LLC, of Washington County, announced the financing Tuesday, which will come from Indiana-based First Commonwealth Bank, Ameriserv Financial, First United Bank, The ERECT Fund, S &amp;amp; T Bank, Tri-State Capital Bank, Parkvale Savings Bank and WesBanco Bank. The project in line to receive $7.25 million from the state and a 10-year, 50 percent tax abatement capped at $825,000 will be built on a 116-acre portion of the North Field site at the Pittsburgh International Airport. Construction is expected to be complete by early 2010.
