FOUNDER and managing director of Blidworth firm Mechanician Ltd Phillip Peat-Bailey joins Barclays Bank relationships manager Mick Mumford, left, to celebrate 25 years of keeping vehicles on the road at his repair company.  The Burma Road firm was founded in 1983 by managing director Phillip and specialises in repairing and testing heavy goods vehicles.  Phillip has banked with Barclays since then and Mick presented the firm with a celebratory bottle of champagne to help it celebrate the milestone anniversary.  Loyalty  Said Mick: 'Phil has grown and developed the business over the years and identified niches in the market place.  'He offers an excellent apprenticeship scheme and this is demonstrated by the loyalty he receives from his staff. I wish them well for the next 25 years!'
