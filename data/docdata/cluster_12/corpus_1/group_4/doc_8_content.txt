Article from: AAP  Font size: Decrease Increase  : Email  : Print  Westpac says it has a credit exposure of about $200 million to childcare provider ABC Learning Centres.  ABC Learning has gone into voluntary administration and its bankers have appointed receivers and managers.  'Following the appointment of voluntary administrators and receivers to ABC Learning, Westpac today confirmed it has exposure to ABC of approximately $200 million,'' the bank said in a statement.  'Westpac will review the implications of the appointment of the voluntary administrators to ABC and adjust existing provisions as required.''  Westpac said it would work with the receivers, the banking syndicate and the government on measures to minimise any disruption to ABC's child care operations.
