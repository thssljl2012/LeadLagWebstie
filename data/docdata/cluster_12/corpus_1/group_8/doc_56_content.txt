SYDNEY, March 18 (Reuters) - Australia Post said on Wednesday it would sell a minimum of A$250 million ($166 million) in five-year notes.   The issue is expected to price around 130 basis points above Swap/BBSW. Australia &amp; New Zealand Banking Group and Commonwealth Bank of Australia are joint-lead managers to the transaction, which is anticipated to price on or before March 19, for settlement on March 25.  The transaction will be rated triple-A.  ($1=1.51 Australian Dollar)  (Reporting by Wayne Cole)
