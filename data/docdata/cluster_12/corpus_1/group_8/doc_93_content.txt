SYDNEY, March 12 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank (NAB.AX), Australia's largest lender, on Thursday became the second Australian bank to announce a dividend cut, saying it would reduce its first-half payout by about 25 percent.  It also announced, in a strategy update, that its main focus would continue to be the domestic market but said it still remained committed to its UK businesses.  (Reporting by Mette Fraende, Editing by Mark Bendeich)
