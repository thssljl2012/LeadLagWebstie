- Boca Raton (FL), USA  With its share price sliding alarmingly towards $0.50, Office Depot has sought to rekindle confidence ahead of an investor conference tomorrow.  In a press release on the eve of Depot's participation at the Bank of America and Merrill Lynch Consumer Conference in New York, the troubled office supplier said that its first quarter North American Retail performance was trending 'slightly better' than the fourth quarter and that EBIT would be 'significantly better', tracking towards a loss of $30-$40 million.  The company also provided reassurance on its cash and liquidity position.  The market reacted positively to the news, with Office Depot's share price soaring by over 40 percent in early trading, but still below the $1 mark.
