SYDNEY, March 27 (Reuters) - Bank of New Zealand (BNZ), a unit of National Australia Bank, has priced NZ$50 million ($28.77 million) of five-year retail bonds at 175 basis points over swap, the bank said on Friday.  The offer, which was not guaranteed by the government of New Zealand, was mostly sold to retail investors.  This was the third issue by the bank this year, following NZ$285 million worth of bonds backed by the government of New Zealand.
