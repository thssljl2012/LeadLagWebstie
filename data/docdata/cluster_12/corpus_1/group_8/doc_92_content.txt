March 17 (Reuters) - Pharmaceutical company Pfizer Inc on   Tuesday sold $13.5 billion of senior notes in five parts, said   IFR, a Thomson Reuters service.   Bank of America, Barclays, Citigroup, Goldman Sachs, and   J.P. Morgan were the active bookrunners for the sale, while   Credit Suisse, Deutsche Bank, HSBC, RBS Greenwich Capital,   Mitsubishi, UBS, and Santander were the passive bookrunners.
