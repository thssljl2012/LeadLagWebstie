National Australia Bank has appointed its group chief financial officer, Mark Joiner, to the bank's board of directors.  Mr Joiner has been with NAB since 2006, when he joined as executive general manager of the office of chief executive Australia.  He was then appointed group executive general manager, Development and New Business, before being appointed CFO in October, 2007.  Mr Joiner's board appointment takes effect on Thursday, March 12.
