Abstracted from The Age  The ANZ Bank is reassessing its expansion in Asia. It may have to write down the value of some its stakes in Asian banks because of the fall in global financial stocks. The structure of its portfolio could be changed to focus on markets with faster growth. It has spent $A166 million to increase its holding in Indonesia's PT Panin Bank  Distributed by News Bites.
