Jan 29 (Reuters) - AT&amp;T Inc on Thursday sold $5.5 billion of notes in three parts, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters service.  Bank of America, Goldman Sachs, and JP Morgan were the bookrunning managers for the 5-year tranche. Bank of America, Citigroup, and JP Morgan were the bookrunning managers for the 10-year tranche. Citigroup, Goldman Sachs, and JP Morgan were the bookrunning managers for the 30-year tranche.
