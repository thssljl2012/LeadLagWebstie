SYDNEY -(Dow Jones)- Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd. (ANZ) has raised A$2.6 billion through the sale of a five-year, government-guaranteed bond, a person familiar with the matter said Friday.  The notes were priced at 110 basis points over the relevant swap rate.
