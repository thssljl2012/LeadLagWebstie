Jan 29 (Reuters) - ConocoPhillips on Thursday sold $6.0 billion of notes in three parts, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters service. Bank of America, Barclays, and Credit Suisse were the active joint bookrunning managers. Citigroup, Deutsche Bank and RBS Greenwich Capital were the passive joint bookrunning managers for the sale.
