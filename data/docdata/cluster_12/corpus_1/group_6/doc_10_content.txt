MELBOURNE -(Dow Jones)- Alumina Ltd. (AWC.AU) said Friday its Chief Financial Officer   Ken Dean  will retire at the end of the first quarter of 2009 to be replaced by former Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd. (ANZ.AU) executive   Judith Downes  .  Downes was most recently chief financial officer of ANZ's institutional division and has been a director of ING Australia and a member of the international Standards Advisory Council.  Alumina Chief Executive   John Bevan  said he was extremely pleased with Downes' appointment and also thanked Dean for his contribution to the company.  Dean has been Alumina's CFO since October 2005 and now plans to increase his involvement in other business activities and continue in his role as a non- executive director of Santos Ltd.
