Australia's biggest lender, the COMMONWEALTH BANK OF AUSTRALIA LTD (ASX:CBA), has split its business and corporate division from the institutional banking and markets areas to service customers better. The new division will include agribusiness and private client services. * The bank said in a statement it had appointed group head of strategy Ian Narev as the new chief of the business and private banking division. Mr Narev will begin his role on January 27.
