Australia and New Zealand Banking Group has appointed Christine Ip as the new CEO for China reporting to the company's CEO for North East Asia, Alistair Bulloch.  Ms Ip will succeed Andrew McGregor who will retire in February 2009 following a 41-year career with Australia and New Zealand Banking Group (ANZ).  Ms Ip joins ANZ from Standard Chartered Bank where she was most recently country head of consumer banking for China. She will commence with ANZ on January 12, 2009 based in Shanghai. The appointment is subject to regulatory approval.  Mr Bulloch said: 'Christine brings significant experience in China as well as considerable experience in consumer and corporate banking. With her extensive knowledge of the region she is ideally placed to lead ANZ's growth in China.'
