David Church has been given the top real estate investment banking job in Europe at Bank of America following the bank?s purchase of rival Merrill Lynch.  Church will be running the real estate gaming and lodging group across Europe, the Middle East and Africa (EMEA), reporting to global real estate head Ron Sturzenegger in the US and to EMEA head of investment banking Mark Aedy.  Church, who joined Bank of America in 2006, to work alongside Simon Hampton in the real estate team, got the top job ahead of his counterpart at Merrill Lynch, Federico Aliboni, head of consumer, retail and real estate origination in EMEA.  The other positions within the expanded real estate team in Europe have yet to be decided.
