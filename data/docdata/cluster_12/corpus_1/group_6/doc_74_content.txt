US Trust, Bank of America Private Wealth Management, as Trustee of the Cross Timbers Royalty Trust (NYSE: CRT), operator of an express trust, announced yesterday (20 January) a cash distribution of beneficial interest of USD0.222855 per unit.  The distribution will be paid on 13 February 2009, to all unit holders of record on 30 January 2009.  The underlying oil and gas sales and average prices attributable to the current month distributions are oil volumes of 19,000 Bbls and gas of 200,000 Mcf at an average price of USD57.24 per Bbl and USD7.11 per Mcf and prior month distributions as per oil volumes of 19,000 Bbls and gas of 151,000 Mcf at an average price of USD72.41 per Bbl and USD9.13 per Mcf.  XTO Energy Inc advised the trustee that lower oil prices for November production resulted in higher costs and lower revenues on properties underlying the Texas Working Interest and this pressure on realised oil prices and monthly distributions is expected to continue.
