(The Australian Financial Review - ABIX via COMTEX) -- MQBKY   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating -- Macquarie Group achieved a 27 per cent rise in retail deposits during the final quarter of 2008. As at the end of December, the firm held $A11.9 billion of retail deposits, an improvement on the $A9.4 billion it had in the corresponding period in 2007. Suncorp-Metway held $A12.73 billion of retail deposits for the final quarter of 2009, while the Bank of Queensland and ANZ Banking had $A12.5 billion and $A53.4 billion in deposits respectively.
