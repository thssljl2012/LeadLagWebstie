April 1 (Reuters) - National Fuel Gas Co, an explorer and distributor of natural gas, on Wednesday sold $250 million in 10-year notes, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters service. Bank of America and JP Morgan were the joint bookrunning managers for the sale.
