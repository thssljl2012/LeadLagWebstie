Dec 9 (Reuters) - FPL Group Capital Inc, a subsidiary of FPL Group Inc, on Tuesday sold $450 million of seven-year senior notes, according to IFR, a Thomson Reuters publication. The size of the deal was increased from an originally planned $250 million. Banc of America Securities, Citigroup Global Markets, Deutsche Bank Securities and Mizuho Securities were the joint bookrunning managers for the sale.
