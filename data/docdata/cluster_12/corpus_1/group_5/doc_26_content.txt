Company Website: http:www.palmharbor.com/DALLAS -- (Business Wire)  Palm Harbor Homes, Inc. (NASDAQ: PHHM) today announced that it has been notified by Textron Financial Corporation (TFC), the Companys floor plan lender, that Textron Inc. has approved a plan to sell all of TFCs commercial financial business through a combination of orderly liquidation and selected sales. As of September 26, 2008, the outstanding balance of the Companys floor plan was $65.5 million.   Larry Keener, chairman and chief executive officer of Palm Harbor Homes, Inc., remarked, This announcement was not totally unexpected news in light of the current economic crisis. We have been in discussions with the management of TFC in conjunction with this announcement and they have informed us that they intend to honor their committed credit lines.   Palm Harbor Homes is one of the nation's leading manufacturers and marketers of factory-built homes. The Company markets nationwide through vertically integrated operations, encompassing manufacturing, marketing, financing and insurance. For more information on the Company, please visit www.palmharbor.com.
