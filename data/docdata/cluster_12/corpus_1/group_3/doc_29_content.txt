THE chairman of energy provider Scottish &amp;amp; Southern Energy has been named as Scotlands leader of the year. Lord Robert Smith, also chairman of Weir Group, was given the honour at the Business Insider Elite Awards ahead of Scottish &amp;amp; Southern chief executive Ian Marchant. In a double success for the company Mr Marchant won in the category businessman of the year. An audience of over 400 gathered at Edinburghs Prestonfield for the ceremony, which featured an address by former Conservative Party leader Michael Howard.Other winners included Susan Rice, chief executive of Lloyds TSB Scotland, who was named businesswoman of the year, and Chris Tiso, the managing director of outdoor clothing specialist Tiso Group, who won the young businessman of the year category.
