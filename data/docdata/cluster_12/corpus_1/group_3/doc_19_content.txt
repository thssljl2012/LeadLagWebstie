Joint statement from:" Dr. Dean Hirsch, Chief Executive Officer, World Vision International" Irene Khan, Secretary General, Amnesty International " Jeremy Hobbs, Executive Director, Oxfam International" Tom Miller, Chief Executive Officer, Plan International" Gerd Leipold, International Executive Director, Greenpeace Last week the US government provided another bailout of $37.8 billion to the giant insurance company, AIG, bringing the total of rescue loans to that one company in the last two weeks to nearly $123 billion. This is $18 billion more than the annual amount of aid to poor countries and twice that needed to achieve the internationally agreed Millennium Development Goals.
