The  reports: The bankers, Joseph Sauvage, Todd Guenther and Jack Paris, who had been brought into Barclays Capital as a part of its purchase of Lehman, are expected to start work at Citi next month. Veteran banker Sauvage has been brought on as a vice chairman of investment banking at Citi, reporting to co-heads of global banking Alberto Verme and Ray Maguire. Guenther was named a managing director and Paris was named director. Both report to Kirk Andrews, head of Citi's North-American power team. Reps at Citi and Barclays confirmed the hires but declined to comment further.
