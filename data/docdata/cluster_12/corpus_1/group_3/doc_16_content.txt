 Dublin - Research and Markets (http://www.researchandmarkets.com/research/c21663/insurance-australi) has announced the addition of the 'Insurance Australia Group Limited: Strategic Corporate Assessment - Strategy, SWOT Analysis and 5-year Financial Insights with In-depth Company Profile' companyprofile to their offering. Insurance Australia Group (IAG) is a leading general insurance group based in Australia with revenues of A$7,935 million (approximately $6236.3 million) in the fiscal year 2007. This profile offers a comprehensive review of IAGs operations and performance. Scope - Contains corporate strategy, value chain presence and SWOT Analysis - Provides detailed business description, segment analysis, 5-year financial trends, key products and key competitors - Includes information on suppliers/ partners, shareholding structure and key employees with biographies Highlights of this title Insurance Australia Group (IAG) primarily underwrites general insurance and is also engaged in related businesses. IAG operates through a network of 320 service centers and branches in Australia; and 40 branches and sales centers in New Zealand. IAG has been expanding into other locations to diversify its business internationally. In the fiscal year 2007, AIG entered the United Kingdom general insurance market through the acquisitions of Equity Insurance Group in January 2007 and Hastings in September 2006. IAGs revenue has been increasing over the past few years. The revenues of IAG increased at a CAGR of 11% during 2003-2007 to reach A$7,935 million (approximately $6236.3 million) in 2007. Furthermore, the revenues increased at a rate of 9.3% in 2007 compared with 2006.
