 SHOPMOBILITY in Barrow is to hold a toy and jigsaw sale to raise money to fix their faulty electric door.Furness Shopmobilitys electric front door was vandalised in August and will cost up to 7,000 to replace.The group is now organising a sale of jigsaws and toys and hopes members of the public will support them by donating any unwanted items.The sale will take place at the Shopmobility centre at Oldham Street car park, behind Barclays Bank, on Saturday November 8 from 10am to 2pm.Anybody who wants to donate any toys or jigsaws can drop them in at the centre.
