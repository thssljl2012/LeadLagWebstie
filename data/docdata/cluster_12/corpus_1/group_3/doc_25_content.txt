WILLIAM Hodge, the firm that supplies shorthand writers to the Scottish courts, has sub-leased a unit in Edinburgh's St Andrews Square from Abbey. CB Richard Ellis, which acted for Abbey, said William Hodge had taken 1,172sq ft until November 2015, at a cost of 32,100 a year. William Hodge joins Barclays Bank, Fibernet UK and Tonstate in the red sandstone building at 1-2 St Andrews Square.King Sturge acted on behalf of William Hodge.
