Sheffield United's players want more than 5million in compensation after their relegation from the Barclays Premier League in May 2007.Fifteen players have instructed a leading sports lawyer to pursue their claim for a loss of earnings in the wake of the Carlos Tevez scandal.Sheffield are already demanding more than 30m after an independent FA tribunal concluded that they should be compensated by West Ham.
