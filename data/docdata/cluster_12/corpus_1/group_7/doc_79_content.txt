Elan Corporation (ELN) announced that it will refine its business operations and functions to realize greater efficiencies and deepen its commitment and focus to key and strategic areas. These adjustments, largely driven by changes in the Biopharmaceuticals business, will result in a reduction in Elan's global workforce of ~230 positions, or 14% of Elan's workforce. Co expects it will reduce operating expenses in 2009 by $30-$35 mln and by ~$50 mln in a full year. Severance and related charges are expected to be $15 mln and will be recorded as a charge in the first half of 2009.
