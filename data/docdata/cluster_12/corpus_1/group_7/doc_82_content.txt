SYDNEY, Feb 26 (Reuters) - Westpac Banking Corp has sold A$1.925 billion ($1.25 billion) of five-year notes backed by the Australian government, at 70 basis points over swap and BBSW, it said on Thursday.  The offer, solely led by Westpac, is the bank's first local bond issue with the government guarantee since the guarantee was introduced late last year.  The bond issue consisted of A$975 million in fixed rate notes and A$950 million in floating rate notes.
