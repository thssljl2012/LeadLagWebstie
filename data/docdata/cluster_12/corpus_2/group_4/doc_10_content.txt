 Part 3                                   The ANZ Gothic Bank, (formerly ES &amp;amp;amp; A Bank), 287 Collins Street (north-east corner of Collins and Queen Streets).   Designed by William Wardell, this building has a richly ornate interior, finely worked masonry and meticulous detail.   Considered the finest secular Gothic Revival building in Australia.
