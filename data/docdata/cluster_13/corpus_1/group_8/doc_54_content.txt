 -- A judge has signed off on the stipulated dismissal of a suit by Land Rover against its insurer American Insurance Group Inc. over claims related to an AIG subrogation suit against the automaker to recover $12 million paid in a settlement over a wreck involving a Land Rover vehicle.  Judge Julian Abele Cook Jr. of the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Michigan approved the dismissal with prejudice Thursday....
