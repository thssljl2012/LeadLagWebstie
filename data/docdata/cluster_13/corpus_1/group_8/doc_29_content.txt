PITTSBURGH, March 5 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- Dick's Sporting Goods, Inc. (NYSE: DKS) today announced that management will participate in the Bank of America and Merrill Lynch Consumer Conference on Thursday, March 12th at 10:40 a.m. Eastern Time.  The presentation and slides will be web cast and can be accessed live or as an archived replay from the link on the Dick's Sporting Goods Investor Relations website at www.dickssportinggoods.com/investors.  About Dick's Sporting Goods, Inc.  Dick's Sporting Goods, Inc. is an authentic full-line sporting goods retailer offering a broad assortment of brand name sporting goods equipment, apparel, and footwear in a specialty store environment. As of January 31, 2009, the Company operated 384 Dick's Sporting Goods stores in 39 states primarily throughout the eastern half of the U.S. The Company also owns Golf Galaxy, Inc., a multi-channel golf specialty retailer, with 89 stores in 31 states, ecommerce websites and catalog operations and Chick's Sporting Goods, Inc., which operates 14 specialty sporting goods stores in Southern California.
