AT&amp;T Inc. is improving wireless coverage in North Carolina via the addition of new cellular equipment at 75 sites this year.  Included in the expansion are Mecklenburg, Gaston, Union and Lincoln counties.  Last month the company said it would install new cellular equipment at four sites in the Charlotte area. They are at the Bank of America Corp. (NYSE:BAC) headquarters on East Trade Street in Charlotte, along Independence Boulevard and Highway 51 in Matthews, at Highway 51 and Lebanon Road in southeast Charlotte and along Mount Gallant Road and Museum Road north of Rock Hill. AT&amp;T (NYSE:T) is based in Dallas.
