KERRY has welcomed a new branch of insurance firm Hibernian Aviva.  The new branch is managed by Joanne Mahony and is located at 5 The Square, Tralee.  It is part of Hibernian Avivas ongoing investment in its nationwide branch network, one of the largest insurance retail networks in Ireland.  'Our highly visible presence in the heart of Tralee means that more consumers can avail of our one-stop shop,' said Ms Mahony.  'The Tralee branch is staffed with highly experienced insurance and investment professionals. The branch is also staffed by people living in the area so customers are met with an unrivalled combination of superior industry expertise and real local knowledge.'  Hibernian Aviva is one of Irelands largest and most successful financial institutions and is insurer of one in four cars on Irish roads.  Main News Page  Previous Page
