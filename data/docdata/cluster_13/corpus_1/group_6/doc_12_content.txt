National risk management provider Avizent announced that John D'Alusio has been named Senior Vice President, Senior Claims Officer, overseeing the company's technical and operations divisions. D'Alusio has over 30 years of experience as an insurance businessman with executive management positions in administration, field operations, and claim technical areas at companies such as Liberty Mutual Insurance Company, Home Insurance Company, AIG, Zenith Insurance Company, HNC Software, Gould &amp; Lamb, and Health Advocates/PMSI MSI MSA Services.
