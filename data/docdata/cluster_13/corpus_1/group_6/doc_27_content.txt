: M. Iqbal Munshi has been elected unopposed General Secretary of Karachi Stock Exchange Employees Union CBA for the years 2009-2010. Iqbal Munshi is also Senior Vice President of Pakistan Banks, Insurance, Financial and Commercial Employees Federation (PBIFCEF) and Finance Secretary of Pakistan Workers Federation (PWF) Karachi Sindh Region.  He is Law graduate and holding diploma of Industrial Relations and Labour Welfare of Nilat.-PR
