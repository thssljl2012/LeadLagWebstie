Bank of America Corporation (NYSE:BAC), a financial institution, declared on 22 January that the Online registration for the 2009 Bank of America Chicago Marathon will open to the public beginning at midnight (CST) on 1 February at http://www.chicagomarathon.com. A The 26.2-mile race will be held on 11 October, beginning and ending in Chicago's historic Grant Park, a tradition going on for 32 years. Registration is open to the first 45,000 registered participants. Before the race, a two-day Health &amp; Fitness Exposition will be held at McCormick Place on 9 and 10 October.
