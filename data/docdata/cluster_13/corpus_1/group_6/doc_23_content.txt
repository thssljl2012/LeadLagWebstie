SPRINGFIELD, Ore. -- A man who claimed he had a gun robbed a bank in Springfield this morning, and police think the suspect is the same man who robbed a bank in California earlir this month.  The Asian male in his 20s entered the Bank of America branch on Pioneer Parkway at one minute past 11 a.m., demanded money -- and claimed to have a gun.  The suspect was wearing a sweatshirt with a skull on it, police said.  Police believe the same man robbed another bank in Modesto, Calif., on Jan. 5.
