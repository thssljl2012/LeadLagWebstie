The Republican secretary of state will take over for early next year, when Democratic Gov. Janet Napolitano vacates the office to serve as U.S. secretary of homeland security in the Obama administration. Brewers transition team includes: Bill Post, CEO of Pinnacle West Capital Corp. Drew Brown, president of real estate development company DMB Associates. Richard Silverman, general manager of Salt River Project. Dan Lewis, vice president of Bank of America. Eileen Klein, vice president of United Healthcare. Chuck Coughlin and Doug Cole of political consulting firm Highground Inc. Bas Aja of the Arizona Cattlemans Association. Connie Wilhelm, president of the Home Builders Association of Central Arizona.
