Police say two bank robberies have occured Wednesday.  Police said a robbery occured at a Bank Of America branch near Juan Tabo and Menaul Boulevards Wednesday morning. Reports said an FBI agent was in the bank as a customer at the time of the robbery.  Police later said a Bank Of Albuquerque branch at 4700 Montgomery Blvd. was also robbed at approximately 1:20 p.m.  Police said a potential suspect from the first heist has been spotted at 900 Eubank Blvd.  Log onto koat.com for updates and stay with Action 7 News for further details.
