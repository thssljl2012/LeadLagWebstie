Montgomery County Police are searching for a man who robbed a Bank Of America in Germantown early this morning.  At approximately 9:30 Saturday morning an unknown male suspect entered the bank and passed a note to the teller demanding money. The teller complied and the suspect fled in an unknown direction.  Bank surveillance cameras were able to capture a picture of the suspect. He is described a white male in his 40's, 5'6' tall and weighing 160 pounds. He was wearing a grey/green long-sleeve plaid shirt, blue jeans, and a light-colored baseball cap.  Anyone with information about this suspect is asked to call the Major Crimes Division Robbery Section at 240-773-5100.
