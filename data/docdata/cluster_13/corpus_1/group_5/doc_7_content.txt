Residents left homeless by an apartment fire in Burnsville are getting checks for 17 thousand dollars per unit this morning, thanks to the holiday spirit of an anonymous donor.In all, the donor wrote a check for $1 million for almost 200 residents ofThe Burncliff apartment building which was destroyed Monday.You can help, too.Food and clothing donations can be brought to Mankato's Old Main Village. That's located at 301 South Fifth Street.Cash or check donations will also be accepted at US Bank, the Red Cross, and the Salvation Army.Just write 'Burncliff Apartment Fire' in your check's Memo line.
