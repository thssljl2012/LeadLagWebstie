The second defendant accused of robbing a Paso Robles bank pleaded not guilty Friday.  Tino Ortega Simmons, 32, of Bakersfield, was arraigned on charges of felony robbery, kidnapping to commit robbery and false imprisonment, according to court officials.  Simmons and Thomas Cameron Kincade, 48, of Fontana, are accused of robbing a Paso Robles Citibank at 1601 Spring St. on June 24.
