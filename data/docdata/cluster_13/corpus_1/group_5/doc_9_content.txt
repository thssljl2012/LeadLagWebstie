CHARLOTTE  Shareholders of Merrill Lynch and Bank of America on Friday approved the investment bank's sale to Bank of America, a move that will create the nation's largest financial-services firm.  The sale of Merrill Lynch brings to an end the independence of a firm founded in 1914. 'We think the crown jewel is the wealth management piece. said Bank of America CEO Ken Lewis. 'It puts us in a completely different league.'
