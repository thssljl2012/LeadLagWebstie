Milwaukee Brewers first baseman Prince Fielder has been named Bank of America Presents the National League Player of the Week for the period of September 15-21, 2008. Bank of America, the Official Bank of Major League Baseball, is the presenting sponsor of the National League and American League Player of the Week Awards, which reflect Bank of America's long-standing tradition of promoting and recognizing higher standards of accomplishment.Last week Fielder led the N.L. with a 1.038 slugging percentage and 27 total bases, and tied for the league lead with 12 hits, six doubles and 11 RBI. The 24-year-old batted .462 (12-26), recorded a .533 on-base percentage and scored six runs. Fielder, who currently has a 12-game hitting streak (20-46, .435), recorded four multi-hit games and three multi-RBI games last week. The left-handed hitting Fielder posted a 3-for-5 game with two home runs, a double and three RBI at Wrigley Field on September 16th. Prince then collected three hits, including a pair of doubles, and three RBI on the following day at Chicago. This marks Fielder's second career weekly award honor (previous: week ending 5/6/07).Other nominees this past week included Fielder's teammate J.J. Hardy (.480, 1 3B, 3 RBI); Florida's Alfredo Amezaga (.391, 1 HR, 8 RBI), John Baker (.440, 5 2B, 7 RBI), Jorge Cantu (.385, 3 HR, 10 RBI), Mike Jacobs (.409, 3 2B, 8 RBI) and Cameron Maybin (.476, 10 H, 7 R); Philadelphia's Ryan Howard (.333, 2 HR, 8 RBI), Brad Lidge (4 SV, 0.00 ERA, 8 SO) and Shane Victorino (.440, 2 HR, 3 RBI); Ryan Dempster (2-0, 2.45 ERA, 10 SO), Ted Lilly (2-0, 2.57 ERA, 14 SO) and Aramis Ramirez (.450, 2 HR, 5 RBI) of the Cubs; San Diego's Adrian Gonzalez (.370, 3 HR, 11 RBI) and Chris Young (1-0, 0.00 ERA, 5 SO); Arizona's Stephen Drew (.407, 2 HR, 4 RBI) and Dan Haren (2-0, 1.80 ERA, 16 SO); Cincinnati's Jay Bruce (.813 SLG, 3 HR, 4 RBI) and Aaron Harang (1-0, 0.00 ERA, 4 SO); Pittsburgh's Ryan Doumit (.400, 1 HR, 8 RBI) and Adam LaRoche (.857 SLG, 4 HR, 8 RBI); San Francisco's Bengie Molina (.367, 2 HR, 6 RBI) and Pablo Sandoval (.429, 4 2B, 6 RBI); Manny Ramirez (.480, 2 HR, 8 RBI) and Derek Lowe (1-0, 0.64 ERA, 10 SO) of the Dodgers; and Colorado's Chris Iannetta (.421, 3 HR, 6 RBI).Tourneau, the world's largest watch store, will award Prince Fielder with a luxury Swiss Timepiece, suitably engraved, in recognition of his accomplishments as Bank of America Presents the National League Player of the Week.Source: http://mlb.mlb.com/news/press_releases/press_release.jsp?ymd=20080922&amp;content_id=3527394&amp;vkey=pr_mlb&amp;fext=.jsp&amp;c_id=mlb
