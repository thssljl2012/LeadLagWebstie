Police are at the scene of a bank robbery in Kaitaia. A man walked into ANZ on Commerce St at around 3.50pm. Inspector Andrew Brill says he passed a note to the teller saying he had a gun, before making off with a quantity of cash. There were five staff members in the bank and four customers, but no one was hurt. Mr Brill says it is not yet clear how the offender made off. &gt;&gt;   2008 NZCity, NewsTalkZB
