John Desko (Photo courtesy suathletics.com) Syracuse, New York (WSYR-TV) - John Desko has won four national championships as head lacrosse coach at Syracuse, and those have led to a special honor for Desko on Monday night. Desko will be officially enshrined into the Greater Syracuse Sports Hall of Fame.He headlines the class of 2008, along with five other honorees, including CBA baseball coach Tom Dotterer, former Major Leaguer Chris Jones, and college basketball official Mike Kitts.The ceremony takes place at 7:00 p.m. at Drumlins.
