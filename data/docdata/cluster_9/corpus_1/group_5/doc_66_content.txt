A FORMER Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) governor is missing.  Sir Harold Knight, 89, was last seen at about 8pm (AEDT) on Friday at an aged care facility on Sydney's north shore.  It is believed Sir Harold, an RBA governor during the Whitlam and Fraser governments, left the Sydney Street, Willoughby, home on foot.  Police and his family hold grave concerns for Sir Harold, who has a medical condition.  He was last seen wearing a grey sports jacket, blue shirt, grey and black diagonally striped tie, grey trousers and bedroom slippers.  Anyone with information about his whereabouts is urged to contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000.
