 Right now Rockford police are looking for the suspect who robbed the US Bank on East State.   It happened around 5 p.m. Police say a man entered the bank, approached the teller and said he had a gun. He then demanded cash and took off on foot. Nobody was injured.   The suspect is described as a black man between 20 and 30 years old. He was wearing a black jacket and blue jeans at the time of the robbery.   If you have any information, contact Rockford Area Crime Stoppers at 815-963-7867
