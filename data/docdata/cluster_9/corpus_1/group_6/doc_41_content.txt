A report from the last day of racing at the Commonwealth Bank 2009 Australian Etchells Championships  At the final day of the Commonwealth Bank 2009 Australian Etchells Championships, hosted by the Royal SA Yacht Squadron, the wind and waves provided tough and challenging conditions.  In the final race, after a general recall the second start was clean away. The fleet split with a poor start for Ian Johnson and his Bananas in Pyjamas. At the top mark, the leaders came in on port, with John Bertrand's Triad crossing ahead.
