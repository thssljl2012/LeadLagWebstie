A Bank of America branch was robbed in Wytheville Friday.  It happened just before 4 p.m. along East Main Street. Police are looking for a man in his early 40's, wearing a toboggan and dark Carhart-like jacket.  They wouldn't say how much the robber got away with. Police say the suspect told bank employees he had a weapon, but did not show one.  Call Wytheville Police with any information.
