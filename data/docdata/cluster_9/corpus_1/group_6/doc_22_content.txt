Plano police are asking for the publics help in identifying two men who robbed a Bank of America branch in the 1900 block of Dallas Parkway on Tuesday shortly before 3 p.m.   One of the robbers, who is described by police as a black man wearing a black baseball cap, a brown hooded sweat shirt and blue jeans, demanded money from a teller and left the bank with an undisclosed amount of cash. Witnesses told police that he made his getaway on the back of a red, white and blue motorcycle driven by a heavy-set black male with a mustache.   Anyone with information about this robbery or either of the suspects is asked to call the Plano Police Department Tip Line at 972-941-2148 or CrimeStoppers at 1-877-373-TIPS.
