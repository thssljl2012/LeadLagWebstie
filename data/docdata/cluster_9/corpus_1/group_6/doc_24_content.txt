Merced police have released a photograph of a man suspected of robbing the Bank of America this week in downtown Merced.  The photograph was taken from a surveillance camera inside of the bank, according to Cmdr. Floyd Higdon. The suspect, who is about 30 years old, is described as a black male, between 5 feet 6 inches and 5 feet 8 inches tall, weighing about 150 pounds. He was wearing a black rain jacket with black jeans, police said.  Police said the suspect has some facial hair and wore a plaid baseball style hat.
