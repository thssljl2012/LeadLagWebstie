DUNCANVILLE  Duncanville police are looking for help to catch a serial bank robber.  Police have released a sketch of the suspect who held up the Compass Bank on Danieldale Road on Tuesday.  The suspect jumped the counter and demanded money before running from the scene.  Investigators think the same man may have robbed the same bank along with a Bank of America branch in December.  Contact Duncanville police if you have any information about this man.
