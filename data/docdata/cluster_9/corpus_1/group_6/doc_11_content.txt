OSHTEMO, Mich. (NEWSCHANNEL 3) - Police are investigating a bank robbery in Oshtemo.  Deputies got a call shortly after 10:30 Tuesday that the Bank of America at 6338 Stadium Drive had been robbed.  Police say that a white male entered the bank, approached a teller and asked for money. He did not show a weapon or imply that he had one. He left the bank on foot heading northwest with an undetermined amount of cash.  The man is described as a white male in his late 20's to early 30's. He is about six feet tall with a stocky build and was wearing a blue knit hat, blue scarf, dark sunglasses, a purple shirt and a coat.  The Kalamazoo Sheriff's Department is working with the FBI on the investigation. If you have any information you are asked to call 269-383-8723 or Silent Observer at 269-343-2100.
