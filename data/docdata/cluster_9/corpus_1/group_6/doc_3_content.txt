Plano police are searching for a man who robbed a bank wearing a dark jacket, button-down shirt, scarf and tie.   According to police, the man walked into a Bank of America on Independence Parkway near Legacy Drive about 2:45 p.m. Monday and, wearing no mask, demanded money from a teller.   He fled in a silver Ford Mustang with an undisclosed amount of cash.   He was described as white and 5-foot-8 with a dark complexion, straight hair, bushy eyebrows and a mustache.   Those with information about the man or the crime are asked to call the Plano Police Department's tip line, 972-941-2148.
