Police are asking for the public's help in finding a man wanted for an attempted bank robbery in Manhattan.  Police say the man walked into a Bank of America at 800 Sixth Avenue around 1:30 p.m. on Saturday.  They say he handed the teller a note demanding cash, but left without any money.  The man is described as being in his 40's, between 5'4' and 5'6', and weighing around 140 pounds.  He was last seen wearing a dark colored jacket and a blue shirt.  Anyone with information is being asked to contact Crime Stoppers by calling 1-800-577-TIPS, by texting TIP577 to CRIMES, or by going to NYPDCrimeStoppers.com.
