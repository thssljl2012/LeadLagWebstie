COLORADO SPRINGS - A U.S. Bank manager in Coloraod Springs is arrested for her involvement in a robbery Saturday morning.  According to Stetson Hills Patrol Officers, 48-year-old Michael Martinez robbed U.S. Bank on Austin Bluffs Parway just before noon. He gave a teller a note that read his has a gun and to give him money. Martinez left with an undisclosed amount of cash.  Martinez was later found and arrested in the area of Oro Blanco and North Carefree.  The bank's manager, 48-year-old Evelyn Solano was also arrested for her involvement in the robbery. Police believe Solano and Martinez were in a relationship and that Solano knew the robbery was going to occur. Solano was working at the bank during the robbery. Police also believe she helped plan the heist.
