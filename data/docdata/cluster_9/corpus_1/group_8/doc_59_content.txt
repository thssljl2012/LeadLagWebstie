DENVER - Congratulations go out Saturday tonight to our colleagues at NEWSCHANNEL 13.  Three employees took top honors at the Colorado Broadcasters Association Awards in Denver.  Reporter Marshall Zelinger and Photojournalist Isaac Blancas won first place in the 'Best News Feature Category' for their story about a local man looking for work on a street corner.  Isaac also won second place in the 'Best Photo Essay' for his story about Broncos fans at training camp.  Rob Namnoum came in second in the category for 'Best Sportscaster.'  NEWSCHANNEL 13 AT 6 was honored with a second place award in the 'Best Newscast Category.
