(KMOV) -- Police are on the scene of a bank robbery in Clayton, MO.  It happened around 11:00 a.m. at the Bank of America in the 8100 block of Forsythe.  News 4 has learned that one person was slightly injured in the robbery.  No word yet on any suspects or how much money they got away with.  Police are not releasing many details at this time.  News 4s Erica Byfield is on the scene and will have more details as soon as they become available.
