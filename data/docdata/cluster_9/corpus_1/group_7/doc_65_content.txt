An unknown assailant robbed a Reno bank Thursday, according to police.  The robber took an undisclosed amount of cash from the US Bank on 301 Vassar Street, about 10:30 a.m., according to Reno police on the scene.  The man said he had a gun, but police said no firearm was seen during the incident.  He is described as a light-skinned man, about 5-feet-4-inches tall, with a light build, about 20 to 30-years-old. He was last seen wearing a dark hoody sweatshirt.  The FBI and Reno Police Department were among those investigating, according to News 4 crews.  Stay with News 4 and KRNV.com for updates.
