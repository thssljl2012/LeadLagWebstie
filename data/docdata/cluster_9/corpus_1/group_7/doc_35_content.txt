 p.m.  Santa Clarita Valley Sheriff's deputies are searching for a man who used a fake gun Wednesday afternoon to rob a Bank of America branch in Canyon Country, a sheriff's deputy said. The man entered the Bank of America in the 19100 block of Soledad Canyon Road just before 2:40 p.m., Lt. Tom Bryski said. The man pointed a fake gun at a teller and demanded an undisclosed amount of money, he said. The suspect, described as a white male, standing 5 feet, 5 inches tall, with a goatee and wearing a gray beanie, fled in a blue car with multi-colored graphics, Bryski said. Anyone with more information about this crime is asked to contact the Santa Clarita Sheriff's Station at (661) 255-1121.
