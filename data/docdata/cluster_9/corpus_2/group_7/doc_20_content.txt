BRIEF: Well-dressed robber hits Irving bank [Fort Worth Star-Telegram, Texas] Knight Ridder/Tribune    "Business News " Feb. 19 --IRVING -- A man dressed in a shirt and tie robbed an Irving bank Wednesday afternoon of an undisclosed amount of money and then fled in a car, police said. Investigators believe the robber had a female accomplice who was waiting in a car. The holdup happened about 2:30 p.m. at Bank of America , 110 W. Texas 114. Witnesses told police that the man entered the bank alone, approached a teller and presented a note that demanded money. The man took the money and placed it in a dark-colored cloth bag, police said. After getting the cash, the man walked out of the bank and into a gold or champagne Pontiac Grand Am. Police released photographs of the man taken from bank surveillance camera. Anyone with information should call Irving police at 972-721-2518.
