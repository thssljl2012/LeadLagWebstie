The summer squash scramble is from a &amp;quot;healthy&amp;quot; cookbook ...        Summer Squash Scramble        From the  Stir Fry Etc.  Meals for Life  from the Kitchens of Healthy Choice Foods, 1996.        1 medium onion, coarsely chopped (1 cup)    1 clove garlic, minces    2 teaspoons olive oil    2 medium zucchini squash, cut into -inch slices (2 cups)    1 yellow squash, cut into -inch slices (1 cup)     teaspoon dried basil leaves     teaspoon salt    2 medium tomatoes, chopped (2 cups)    1 to 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)        Combine onion, garlic and oil in a 10-inch nonstick skillet.  Cook over medium-high heat for 2 to 3 minute, or until onion is tender, stirring frequently.        Add squashes, basil, and salt.  Cook for 5 to 7 minutes, or until squash are tender-crisp, stirring constantly.        Stir in tomatoes.  Cook for 1 to 2 minutes, or until hot, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
