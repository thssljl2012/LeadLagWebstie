(RTTNews) - Citigroup (C) finished at its best level in nearly eight weeks on an encouraging memo from its CEO. Shares finished at $17.58,m up $5.08 for the session.  Citigroup CEO Vikram Pandit said in a letter to employees that the company is profitable through the first two months of 2009 and is having its best quarter-to-date performance since the third quarter of 2007.  Other banking stocks also surged on the news. Bank of America (BAC   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating) jumped $1.04 to $4.79, JP Morgan Chase (JPM   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating) added $3.60 to $19.50 and , PNC Financial Services (PNC   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating) rose $4.99 to $24.71.  The Kbw Bank Index rose 15.6 percent on the day, extending yesterday's advance to take the index further off the multi-year closing low that it set last Friday.
