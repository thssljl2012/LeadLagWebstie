Bank of America Corporation   As if Bank of America Corporation didn't have enough reasons already to rue the day it agreed to acquire Merrill Lynch, the bank is now being sued by 5 public pension funds over the deal. The class action suit alleges that BAC made 'untrue statements' preceding its purchase of Merrill Lynch, and neglected to provide material information to shareholders.
