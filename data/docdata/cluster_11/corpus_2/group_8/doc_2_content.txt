Analyst Actions: Macy's Upgraded at B of A and Goldman Sachs   10:21 AM, Mar 6, 2009 -- Macy's Inc. (M) shares are getting a lift this morning after shares were upgraded at both Bank of America/Merrill Lynch (BAC) and Goldman Sachs (GS). Shares are up 7.9% to $7.11.       Macy's was upgraded at Bank of America/Merrill Lynch to "neutral" from "underperform" and $8 price target maintained. The analyst believes that after the recent stock drop, consensus and management's expectation are realistic and current valuation is appropriate.       Shares were also upgraded at Goldman Sachs to "buy" from "neutral" based on decreased balance sheet risk, market share gains, and improved earnings power after cost cutting.
