Bank of America-BAC target lowered to $12 from $23 at RBC Capital 01/14 07:48 AM RBC lowered their estimates for Banc of America to reflect further credit deterioration, the acquisition of Merrill and the TARP. The firm lowered their 2008 estimate to $1.10 from $1.53 and 2009 estimate to $1.55 from $2.45 and maintains a Sector Perform rating.
