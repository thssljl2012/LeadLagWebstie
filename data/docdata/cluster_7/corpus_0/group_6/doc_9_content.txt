Dollar and Yen Fall on New Bank Bailout The dollar and yen fell on Friday after the US government agreed to provide a $138 billion lifeline to Bank of America. Treasury Secretary Henry Paulson said the government is working on the idea of setting up a bad bank to deal with the banking sectors illiquid assets.
