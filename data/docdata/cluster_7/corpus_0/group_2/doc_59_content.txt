PALO ALTO, CA--(Marketwire - September 11, 2008) -  Essex Property Trust, Inc. (NYSE: ESS), a real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests in apartment communities located in highly desirable, supply-constrained markets, announced today that Keith R. Guericke, President and CEO, and Michael T. Dance, Executive Vice President and CFO, will be presenting at the Bank of America 38th Annual Investment Conference on Monday, September 15, 2008. The presentation is scheduled for 10:00 a.m. Pacific Daylight time with the breakout session immediately following.
