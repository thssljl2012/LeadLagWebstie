Retail sales fell 1.1% in September, more than twice the forecast (FT, WSJ).  House prices fell 1.8% in the third quarter.  The most recent reading of the manufacturing index fell sharply to its lowest level since 1992.  An ANZ Bank survey found that the total number of jobs advertised in newspapers and on the internet declined 5.9% in October.  ANZ is predicting that unemployment will rise to 6.5% by 2010 from 4.3% in September.The rising tide of negative economic data has convinced analysts that the central bank will cut rates by a half a percent when it meets Tuesday.
