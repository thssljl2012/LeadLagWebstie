Small businesses look set to benefit from the interest rate cuts. Bank overdrafts are linked to Bank of England base rates so there is no reason why the rates should fall in line with today's 1.5 point cut to 3pc. Barclays has confirmed that it is passing on the cut "immediately" through to its business customers with overdrafts.
