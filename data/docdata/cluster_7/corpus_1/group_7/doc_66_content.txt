CANBERRA, Feb 20 (Reuters) - Australia's central bank has no concerns about Australia's sovereign credit rating, and said on Friday major banks had continue to lend strongly to the corporate sector. 'Australia's sovereign credit rating is very strong. Our debt position is among the very best in the world. The sovereign position is incredibly strong,' Reserve Bank of Australia Deputy Governor Ric Battellino told a parliamentary hearing.   (Reporting by James Grubel)
