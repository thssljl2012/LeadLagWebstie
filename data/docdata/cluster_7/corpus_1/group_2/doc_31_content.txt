'AFR Smart Investor' magazine has created a 'Cash Club'. This is a list of stocks in Australia which reliably deliver good dividends. This is a good source of income for investors and these stocks are recommended for those who want a predictable income. 'AFR' reviews those on the list regularly and discards any which fail to deliver. The stocks in the Club in October 2008 include Adelaide Brighton, Alesco, AMP, ANZ Bank, ASX, Caltex Australia, ConnectEast, David Jones, DUET, Macquarie Group, National Australia Bank, Pacific Brands, Platinum Asset Management, QBE Insurance, Stockland, Count Financial, InvoCare, IRESS Market Technology, Treasury Group and Wotif.com. These stocks should be able to weather the current market volatilityDistributed by News Bites.
