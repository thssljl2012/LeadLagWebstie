LONDON, Sept 19 (Reuters) - Office of Fair Trading: * The OFT has started a consultation process regarding the acquisition of HBOS by Lloyds TSB (nyse: LYG - news - people ). * The OFT is considering whether arrangements are in progress which, if carried into effect, will result in the creation of a relevant merger situation. * It said that if it does find a relevant merger situation, the OFT will make further considerations as to whether the situation will result in a substantial lessening of competition in the UK or other countries.
