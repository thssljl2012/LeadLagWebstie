SINGAPORE, Sept 24 (Reuters) - British bank Barclays has sold most of its 6.12 percent stake in Berlian Laju Tanker , the Indonesian shipping firm said on Wednesday. Barclays now holds just 0.02 percent of Berlian Laju, which is listed in Jakarta and Singapore.The UK bank did not disclose the selling price, but the stake should be worth about 386 billion rupiah ($41 million) based on the Indonesian firm's closing price of 1,380 rupiah a share.(Reporting by Kevin Lim; Editing by Jennifer Tan) ($1=9340 Rupiah) Keywords: BARCLAYS BERLIAN LAJU/ tf.TFN-Europe_newsdesk@thomson.com vs
