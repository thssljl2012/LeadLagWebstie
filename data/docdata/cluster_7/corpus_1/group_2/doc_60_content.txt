SYDNEY -(Dow Jones)- Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd. (ANZ) said Tuesday it has raised A$1.081 billion in new Tier 1 capital through its convertible preference share offer.In a statement, ANZ said it has issued around 10.81 million convertible preference shares at A$100 each. ANZ said earlier this month it had expected to raise around A$1 billion via the issue.'We are very pleased with the strong investor demand that the offer received,' ANZ Group Treasurer Rick Moscati said.Last week, ANZ raised A$600 million in Tier 1 capital through a hybrid convertible notes issue in the U.S. private placement market.-By Lyndal McFarland, Dow Jones Newswires; 61-3-9650-0637; lyndal.mcfarland@ dowjones.comClick here to go to Dow Jones NewsPlus, a web front page of today's most important business and market news, analysis and commentary: http:// www.djnewsplus.com/al?rnd=ssOOQJa0bVq17iQhEhuI6g%3D%3D.
