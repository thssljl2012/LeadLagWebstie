DJ Australia's Swan: Fiscal Stimulus Boosted Retail Data CANBERRA (Dow Jones)--Australian Treasurer Wayne Swan said Wednesday that a combination of looser monetary policy and fiscal stimulus are working to invigorate the Australian economy, but reiterated that the trade impact from a dramatic slowdown in the global economy will be difficult to avoid. 'Things would be a lot worse if the Australian government and the Reserve Bank of Australia hadn't acted early and acted decisively,' Swan told Australian Broadcasting Corp. radio. 'I think we've seen the beneficial impact of the economic security strategy come through in the retail sales figures for December, and again in the retail sales figures for January,' he said, referring to an initial A$10.4 billion stimulus package unveiled in October.
