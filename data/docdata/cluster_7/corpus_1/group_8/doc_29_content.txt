The Australian dollar has opened lower but was trading in a tight range ahead of the Reserve Bank of Australia's (RBA) March board meeting and interest rate decision.  At 0700 AEDT, the Australian dollar was trading at US$0.6308 11, down from Monday's close of US$0.6335/38.  During offshore trading, the unit moved between a low of US$0.6291 and a high of US$0.6386.
