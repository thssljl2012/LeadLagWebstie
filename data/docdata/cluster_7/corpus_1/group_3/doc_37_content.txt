(CEP News) - Credit from Australian financial intermediaries increased against expectations by 0.7% for the month of September, down from the month prior's +0.5% figure and above the 0.4% increase expected.On an annual basis, private sector credit rose 10.1%, higher than the 9.6% increase forecast but lower than the prior month's 10.1% figure, the Reserve Bank of Australia reported Friday morning (Thursday night EDT).All growth rates released by the RBA are seasonally adjusted.By Megan Ainscow, mainscow@economicnews.caCEP Newswires - CEP News ? 2008.
