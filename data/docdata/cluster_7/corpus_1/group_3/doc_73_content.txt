Australia's third biggest mortgage provider Westpac Banking Corporation has cut its fixed home loan rates by up to 1.1 percentage points.Westpac now has the most competitive fixed rate in the market for three-year mortgages with a rate of 6.99 per cent, the Sydney-based bank said in a statement.The bank cut its one-, four- and five-year fixed rate mortgage rates to 7.19 per cent.Westpac trails Commonwealth Bank of Australia and National Australia Bank in Australia's home loan market.
