National Australia Bank (NAB) is offering existing customers bonus interest upon rollover to its new self service banking distribution channel, dubbed UBank. UBank will operate independently to NAB's other retail brands and target a new set of retail customers, NAB's Star Direct and marketing boss, Greg Sutherland said. Focussed on savings products, UBank is offering Australia's only term deposit paying bonus interest to existing NAB customers upon rollover. As well, UBank will pay 8.5 per cent per annum on its three month term deposit.
