SYDNEY, Oct 7 (Reuters) - Commonwealth Bank of Australia (News)  (CBA) has started exclusive talks with British bank HBOS  about a potential takeover of BankWest, HBOS' Australian operation, CBA said on Tuesday. CBA, Australia's second-largest bank, said no agreement had yet been reached on a deal. HBOS is being taken over by fellow British bank Lloyds TSB  in a UK government-sponsored deal after it ran into funding difficulties amid the global credit crisis.
