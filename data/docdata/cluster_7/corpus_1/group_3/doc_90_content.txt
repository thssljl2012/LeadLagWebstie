CANBERRA -(Dow Jones)- Australian Treasurer Wayne Swan said Tuesday that the government will consider refining its blanket guarantee on bank deposits if needed, as analysts expressed concern the guarantee is distorting financial markets. 'We have said all along that we will consider any necessary refinements to the guarantee, following consultation with industry and further discussions with the regulators,' Swan said in an email.
