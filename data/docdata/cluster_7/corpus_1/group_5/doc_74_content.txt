SYDNEY -(Dow Jones)- Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA.AU) Wednesday said it has raised Y17 billion through the sale of a five-year, Australian government-guaranteed bond. The notes were priced on Tuesday at around 100 basis points over U.S. Libor, a funding official told Dow Jones Newswires. -By Enda Curran, Dow Jones Newswires; 61-2-8235-2958; enda.curran@dowjones.com
