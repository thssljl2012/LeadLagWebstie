Total credit provided to the private sector by financial intermediaries fell 0.3 per cent in December, following a 0.4 per cent increase in November, the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) said.  Over the year to December, total credit rose by 6.7 per cent.  Housing credit rose 0.4 per cent in December, and by 7.6 per cent over the year, seasonally adjusted.  Other personal credit was down 1.1 per cent in December, falling 5.2 per cent over the year.  Business credit fell 1.1 per cent in the month, and increased 8.0 per cent over the year.
