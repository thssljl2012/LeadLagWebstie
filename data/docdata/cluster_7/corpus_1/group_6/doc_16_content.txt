WASHINGTON, Jan 9 (Reuters) - The American Bankers Association in a statement:  * Says it opposes a new plan by Democratic senators, which is supported by Citigroup  (nyse: C -  news  -  people ), that would let federal bankruptcy judges change mortgage terms to help troubled borrowers avoid foreclosure.  * Says plan would mean 'overly broad mortgage cram-down authority' that would hurt U.S. banks.
