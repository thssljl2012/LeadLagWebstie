Font size: Decrease Increase  : Email  : Print  NAB has reduced its standard variable home loan rate by 62 basis points, a day after the Reserve Bank cut official interest rates by 0.75 of a percentage point.  NAB's standard variable mortgage interest rate will fall from 8.36 per cent to 7.74 per cent, effective November 10, the bank says.  The Reserve Bank yesterday cut interest rates by 75 basis points to 5.25, a larger reduction than most economists had predicted.  Westpac and Commonwealth Bank have also announced rate cuts, although none of the banks have passed on the RBA's cut in full.
