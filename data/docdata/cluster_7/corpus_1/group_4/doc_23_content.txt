(Reuters via COMTEX) -- MVITF   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating -- Mirvac Real Estate Investment Trust has refinanced a A$625 million ($417 million) debt facility ahead of its December 2008 expiry, the trust said in a statement on Friday. The new facility matures in two tranches, with half expiring in September 2010 and the remaining half in September 2011.  The debt facility has been refinanced with a syndicate of lenders including Westpac Banking Corp and Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd, Mirvac said.
