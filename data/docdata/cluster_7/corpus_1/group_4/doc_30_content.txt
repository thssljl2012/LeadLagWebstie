SYDNEY, Nov 20 (Reuters) - Westpac New Zealand, a unit of   Australia's Westpac Banking Corp, has priced a NZ$265   million ($145.3 million) five-year bond issue at 130 basis points   over swap, it said on Thursday.   The offer was sold to both retail and institutional   investors.
