Wednesday 29 October 2008 20:00, Barclays Premier LeagueSami Hyypia returns to the Liverpool starting line-up as Rafa Benitez makes four changes for tonight's clash with Portsmouth. The Finland international comes into the side in place of Daniel Agger, while Jermaine Pennant, Lucas Leiva and Ryan Babel also earn call-ups.
