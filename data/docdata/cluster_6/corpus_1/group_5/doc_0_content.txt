CINCINNATI  Despite being outshot 51-23, the Dayton Bombers beat the Cincinnati Cyclones 5-4 in a shootout at U.S. Bank Arena.  The Bombers won the shootout 2-1 on goals by Justin Bowers and Danny LaPointe.  Dayton's Paul Drew made 47 saves in net to earn the win.  Dayton got goals from four different players in the game and were led by Greg Labenski, who had three points (1+2)  The Bombers are off until Friday, when they will play the Cyclones for the third game in a row, this time at the Nutter Center.
