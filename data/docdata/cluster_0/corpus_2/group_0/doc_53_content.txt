       CBA raises rates  Chris Zappone July 11, 2008 - 11:07AM  Commonwealth Bank upped its rates today, making it the third large bank in a week to do so amid higher funding costs.  The cost of a standard variable home loan offered by the bank rose to 9.58% from 9.44%.  St George and BankWest both raised rates on their standard variable home rates in the past week.
