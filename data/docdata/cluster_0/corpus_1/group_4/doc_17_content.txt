(The West Australian - ABIX via COMTEX) -- MTGRF   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating -- The global financial turmoil has resulted in more job losses in Western Australia's (WA) mining sector. Mount Gibson Iron is to cut 190 jobs after Chinese customers defaulted on sales contracts, while engineering group WorleyParsons said 250 employees have been removed from WA mining sites, with 70 of the contractors to lose their jobs. The job losses relate to contracts with Moly Mines and Fortescue Metals Group, which has decelerated the expansion of iron ore production in response to diminished demand from China.
