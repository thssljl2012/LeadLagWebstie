The Reserve Bank of Australia has confirmed it intervened in the foreign exchange market to support the Australian dollar.  An RBA spokeswoman confirmed the central bank intervened on Thursday morning to provide more liquidity into currency markets.  The local dollar opened trading in Australia at 64.09 US cents, down from 66.03 at the close Wednesday.  At 1151 AEDT Thursday, the currency was trading at 64.13 US cents.
