 INTEREST rates are on hold after a decision by the Reserve Bank of Australia to keep the official cash rate steady.The Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) today left official interest rates steady at 7.25 per cent. The central bank last lifted the cash rate in March this year by 25 basis points. Economists were unanimous in their expectation that interest rates would remain unchanged following the RBA board meeting.The rates decision had been widely predicted by market watchers.
