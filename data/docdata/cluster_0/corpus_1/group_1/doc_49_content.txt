 Investors will be keenly anticipating the release of the minutes of the most recent Reserve Bank of Australia board meeting on 19 August 2008. The minutes are likely to give a greater insight into the central bank's decision to leave the official cash rate at 7.25 per cent, following the release of the quarterly monetary policy statement in the week ended 15 August. The futures market has priced in a rate cut of 25 basis points in September, although a 50 basis point cut remains a distinct possibility
