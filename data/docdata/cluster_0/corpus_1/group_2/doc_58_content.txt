The FTSE 100 Index plunged nearly 3% on Monday as Lehman Brothers' bankruptcy move caused turmoil for financial markets worldwide. HBOS was the leading blue chip faller in London, down more than 12%, with Barclays down nearly 9% as investors digested the seismic news from the US.They helped drag the Footsie 147 points lower to 5269.7 after the first hour of trading - a drop of 2.7%.
