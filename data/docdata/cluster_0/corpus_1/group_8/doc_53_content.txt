MEXICO CITY, March 11 (Reuters) - Mexico's peso firmed sharply for the second day in a row on Wednesday, supported by hopes that Washington will act soon to help stabilize U.S. banks.  The peso  strengthened 1.23 percent to 15.085 per dollar. (Reporting by Michael O'Boyle; Editing by Tom Hals)  Keywords: MARKETS MEXICO/PESO
