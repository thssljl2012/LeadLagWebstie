(Updates to market open)  SYDNEY, March 4 (Reuters) - Australian stocks fell 1.7 percent on Wednesday, down for a fourth straight day, as a grim outlook for global financial stocks pushed down domestic banks including Commonwealth Bank of Australia Ltd . The benchmark S&amp;P/ASX 200 index  lost 53.5 points to 3,166.2 by 2311 GMT, after falling to a fresh five-year closing low on Tuesday. ($1=A$1.57) (Reporting by Denny Thomas)
