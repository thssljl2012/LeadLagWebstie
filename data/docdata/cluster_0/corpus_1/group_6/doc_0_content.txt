The latest steps by the government to stem the financial crisis on Friday helped lift the financial sector as well as the broader market, with equities bouncing back for a second session after a string of losses.   The Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJI) climbed 86.34 points, or 1.1%, to 8, 300.74, with 23 of its 30 components posting gains. The S&amp;P 500 (SPX) added 8.09 points, or 1%, to stand at 851.83 and the Nasdaq Composite (RIXF) gained 11.91 points, or 0.8%, to 1,523.75.
