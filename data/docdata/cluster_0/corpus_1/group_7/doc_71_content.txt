Falls in banking stocks have dragged the Australian sharemarket lower this morning, after falls on Wall Street overnight.  The All Ordinaries Index is down 25 points to 3,373.  The ASX-200 is 28 points lower at 3,421.  The Commonwealth Bank, the National Australia Bank and Westpac are down between 1.8 and 2.25 per cent.  ANZ shares are down more than 0.5 per cent.   Shares in Caltex have fallen 9.5 per cent after the company said its 2008 profit had more than halved.  The Australian dollar is up from yesterday's close at 64.56 US cents.
