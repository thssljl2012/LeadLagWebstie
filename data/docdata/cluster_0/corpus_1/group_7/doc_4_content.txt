HONG KONG, Feb 10 (Reuters) - Hong Kong shares will open little changed on Tuesday with investors expected to stay on the sidelines ahead of the announcement of the U.S. bank rescue plan and key economic data from China.   The benchmark Hang Seng Index is indicated to open 18.06 points higher at 13,787.12.   The China Enterprises Index of top mainland firms will open 0.2 percent lower at 7,740.37.
