Oil dropped below $145 a barrel Tuesday in Asia although a series of threats to supply in a finely balanced market continues to keep a floor under prices.'The oil market right now is fundamentally tight, which is why prices have been high and volatile,' said David Moore, a commodity strategist with Commonwealth Bank of Australia in Sydney.In midday trading in Singapore, light, sweet crude for August delivery was down 26 cents at $144.92 a barrel in Asian electronic trading on the New York Mercantile Exchange.
