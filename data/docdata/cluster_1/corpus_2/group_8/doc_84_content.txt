SECURITY DESCRIPTION: MSDW Structured Asset Corp., Structured Asset Trust Unit Repackagings (SATURNS), Bank of America Debenture-Backed, Series 2001-6, 7.25% Callable Units, issued in $25 denominations. Underlying securities are the 8.07% Trust Preferred Capital Securities due 12/31/2026 issued by BankAmerica Institutional Capital A, and guaranteed by Bank of America Corp. (NYSE: BAC). The SATURNS are redeemable anytime at the issuer's option on 30 to 60 days notice at $25 per unit plus accrued and unpaid dividends. If redeemed prior to 12/31/2006 the holders may receive an additional amount of up to $2.50 per unit. Distributions of 7.25% per annum are paid quarterly on 3/31, 6/30, 9/30 &amp; 12/31 to holders of record on 3/15, 6/15, 9/15 &amp; 12/15 respectively. The company has the right, at any time, to defer interest payments for up to 20 consecutive quarters (but not beyond the maturity date). The trust's assets consist of the 8.07%% Junior Subordinated Deferrable Interest Debentures due 12/31/2026 which were purchased from the company using the funds generated from the sale of the trust preferred securities. To be issued the units must be assigned a rating of at least A- by S&amp;P and aa2 by Moody's. Units are expected to trade flat, which means accrued interest will be reflected in the trading price and the purchasers will not pay and the sellers will not receive any accrued and unpaid interest. On 11/8/02 MDSW Structured Asset Corp. changed their name to MS Structured Asset Corp.
