Jetstar Pacific and AIG Vietnam launched a travel insurance plan for online customers Wednesday.  JetCover has been developed specifically for Jetstar passengers on flights originating from Vietnam.  Jetstar Pacific, Vietnams budget airline, said premiums start at US$2.50 for a domestic one-way flight and can coast as much as $9 for an international return trip.  The package covers 12 areas including accidents, medical expenses coverage, flight delays, trip cancellations and baggage losses.  Reported by Vinh Bao
