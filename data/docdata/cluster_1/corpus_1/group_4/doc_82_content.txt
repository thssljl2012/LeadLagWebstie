TWN, AFP TOKYO -- A consortium led by Chinas sovereign wealth fund is in talks with struggling US insurance giant American International Group to acquire a stake in one of its units, a Japanese daily reported Friday. The purchase would open the door for future capital and business tie-ups between AIG and Chinese investors, paving the way for the Asian giant to become a major player in the global insurance market.  AIG and China Investment Corp. aim to reach a conclusion by the end of this year, the Nikkei business daily reported, without naming its sources.
