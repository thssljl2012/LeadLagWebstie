Britain's Serious Fraud Office (SFO) has launched a preliminary inquiry into suspected irregularities at a British subsidiary of American International Group, the SFO said on Thursday.   The SFO said its probe into the UK operations of AIG Financial Products did not concern the insurance operations of AIG in Britain or elsewhere.
