LONDON - - Britain's Serious Fraud Office (SFO) has launched a preliminary inquiry into suspected irregularities at a British subsidiary of  Inc., The SFO said its probe into the UK operations of AIG Financial Products Corp did not concern the insurance operations of AIG in Britain or elsewhere. "It is right for us to look into the UK operations of AIG Financial Products Corp to determine if there has been criminal conduct," Richard Alderman, director of the SFO, said in a statement. "We will use our full range of powers to seek information and to speak to those with an inside knowledge of the company's operations." AIG said it was co-operating fully with the SFO investigation.
