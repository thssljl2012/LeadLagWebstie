Mumbai, Oct. 7Tata AIG Life Insurance Company Limited has launched a unit-linked product called Tata AIG Life InvestAssure, a press release issued by the company said. The features of the product include a lump sum benefit that will be paid for each day of stay in hospital, unlimited number of surgeries and full family coverage with a maximum limit of Rs 21.90 lakh for daily hospitalisation charges for the holders lifetime, the release added.
