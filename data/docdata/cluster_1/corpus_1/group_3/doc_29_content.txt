(Reuters via COMTEX) -- AIG   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating -- Robert Willumstad, a former chief executive of American International Group from June through mid-September 2008, testified at a U.S. House Oversight and Government Reform hearing on Tuesday:* Said 'I was not aware of it and had I been aware of it, I would have prevented it from happening' when asked about a week-long spa retreat taken by several AIG executives a week after the insurance company received an $85 billion bailout from the federal government.* Members of the committee cited invoices showing that the spa retreat included a $200,000 bill for hotel rooms, $150,000 for meals, $23,000 bill for massages and other spa services, and $14,000 for hair salon and manicure services at the St. Regis Resort in Monarch Beach, California.* AIG received a federal loan of up to $85 billion on Sept. 16 after losses in its financial products unit drove it to the brink of collapse.
