  WASHINGTON (AFP) Insurance giant AIG on Monday reported a net loss of 61.7 billion dollars in the fourth quarter of 2008, bringing total losses for the year to nearly 100 billion dollars. 'AIG's results in the fourth quarter were negatively affected by continued severe credit market deterioration,' the company said in a statement.  It said its net loss for 2008 was 99.3 billion dollars, compared to net profits of 6.2 billion dollars for 2007.
