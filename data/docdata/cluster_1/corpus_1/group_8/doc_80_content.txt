NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Fitch Ratings revises ratings assigned to the Calhoun County Navigation District (Formosa Plastic Corp., Texas Project) solid waste disposal revenue bonds series 2000 and 2001 and Calhoun County Navigation District (Formosa Plastic Corp., Texas Project) pollution control revenue bonds series 2002 to 'A+/F1+'. On the effective date of March 27, 2009, the rating will be based on the support of three substitute irrevocable direct-pay letters of credit (LOCs) to be issued by Bank of America, N.A. On such date, Bank of America, N.A. will replace Wachovia Bank, N.A. as the provider of the LOCs supporting the bonds. Wachovia Bank, N.A. will continue as the remarketing agent for the bonds.  The ratings will expire upon the earliest of: April 11, 2010 for the 2000 and 2001 bonds, and March 27, 2010 for the series 2002 bonds, the stated expiration date of the substitute LOCs, unless such dates are extended, any prior termination of the substitute LOCs, or defeasance of the bonds.  Fitch's rating definitions and the terms of use of such ratings are available on the agency's public site, www.fitchratings.com. Published ratings, criteria and methodologies are available from this site, at all times.
