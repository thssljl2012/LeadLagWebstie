Fitch rates Citibank Credit Card Issuance Trust Citiseries class A, (2009-1) notes as follows:   --$3 billion one-month LIBOR + 1.75% class A (2009-1) 'AAA'.   The Rating Outlook is Stable. The rating reflects the available credit enhancement for the class A notes, which totals 16%, achieved through 5.25% subordination of class B notes and 10.75% of class C notes. The rating also reflects the quality of the underlying receivable pool, Citibank (South Dakota), N.A.'s underwriting and servicing capabilities, as well as the transaction's legal structures, which employ early amortization triggers.
