If you were hit with an overdraft fee, returned item fee or similar fee between December of 2000 through December of 2007, you may be eligible to receive up to $78 dollars. It's all part of a class action settlement in which Bank of America is accused of changing the posting order of transactions in order to increase the revenue it received from non-sufficient funds fess, overdraft fees and other similar charges.   For more details on the settlement and how you can make your claim, visit www.clossonsettlement.com.
