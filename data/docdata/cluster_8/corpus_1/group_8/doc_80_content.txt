Photo: Reuters.A bill to be introduced in the the US House of Representatives would impose a an extra tax of 60 percent on large bonuses to be paid to executives at the bailed-out insurance giant AIG. Michigan Democratic Representative Gary Peters drew up the bill and has picked up five co-sponsors, with other lawmakers voicing support on Tuesday for taxing the bonuses that President Barack Obama has termed an 'outrage'. Mr Peters said: 'The legislation I'm proposing will get taxpayers their money back'.
