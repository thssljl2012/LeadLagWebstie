Armed robbers have hit two separate banks in Christchurch this afternoon.Just before 2pm, a Caucasian man entered the ANZ bank at Cathedral Square. He handed the teller a note and a sum of cash was handed over. Just after 2pm, the same thing happened at the Westpac on Riccarton Rd.Police are investigating both incidents, but say it is too early to determine whether they are connected.Anyone who witnessed either robberies is asked to contact police.
