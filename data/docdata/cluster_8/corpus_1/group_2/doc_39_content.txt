 Sep. 15--A McKeesport man has been sentenced in federal court in Pittsburgh to five years in prison followed by five years' probation for operating a bank fraud scheme.Donald Jones, 40, of Church Avenue was convicted of opening an account at First Commonwealth Bank in which he deposited counterfeit checks.
