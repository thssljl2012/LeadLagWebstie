Commonwealth Bank of Australia chief marketing officer, Mark Buckman, will no longer seek to become chair of the Australian Association of National Advertisers. Newspaper publisher News Limited's group marketing and circulation director, Joe Talcott, is now the leading contender for the role. The appointment of a media figure as chair would be controversial. Talcott said he would remove himself from board discussions on issues such as the future of TV advertisingDistributed by News Bites.
