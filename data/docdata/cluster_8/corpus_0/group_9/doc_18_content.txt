Bank Profits Appear Out of Thin Air   This is starting to feel like amateur hour for aspiring magicians. Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan Chase, Citigroup and, on Monday, Bank of America all tried to wow their audiences with what appeared to be  presto!  better-than-expected numbers.
