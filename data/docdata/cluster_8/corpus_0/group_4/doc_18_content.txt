UPSTATE, SC  November 12, 2008  Upstate-based Rosenfeld Einstein, one of the Carolinas fastest-growing insurance organizations, announced that Sarah DeGlau has joined the firms property and casualty group as a Personal Lines Account Manager. Ms. DeGlau is a native of New Alexandria, Pennsylvania, and current resident of Belton, South Carolina.
