NY Times Dealbook: Profits at the Big Banks Appearing Out of 'Thin Air' Ref - http://dealbook.blogs.nytimes.com/2009/04/21/bank-profits-appear-out-of-thin-air/ Another day, another attempt by a Wall Street bank to pull a bunny out of the hat, showing off an earnings report that it hopes will elicit oohs and aahs from the market. Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan Chase, Citigroup and, on Monday, Bank of America all tried to wow their audiences with what appeared to be  presto!  better-than-expected numbers. But in each case, investors spotted the attempts at sleight of hand, and didnt buy it for a second.
