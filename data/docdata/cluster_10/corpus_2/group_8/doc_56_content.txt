Bank of America Uncovers $120 Mln in Additional Merrill Losses: NY Times   7:49 AM, Mar 6, 2009 -- Bank of America (BAC) has recently uncovered at least $120 million of losses attributable to one London trader, and "hundreds of millions" of other losses on derivative trades, The New York Times reports Friday.       According to the report, the losses occurred before Bank of America's rushed acquisition of Merrill Lynch and were missed by bank accountants reviewing Merrill's books.
