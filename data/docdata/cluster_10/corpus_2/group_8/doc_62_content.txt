 US Bankruptcy Filings Jumped 31% in 2008   Topics:Bankruptcy   Economic Data   Consumers   Economy (Global)   Economy (U.S.)By: Reuters   05 Mar 2009   02:38 PM ET Text Size U.S. bankruptcy filings surged 31 percent in 2008 as both businesses and consumers struggled to make ends meet in a worsening economy, according to court data released on Thursday.       Total bankruptcy filings rose to 1,117,771 last year, the Administrative Office of U.S. Courts said, up from 850,912 in 2007.
