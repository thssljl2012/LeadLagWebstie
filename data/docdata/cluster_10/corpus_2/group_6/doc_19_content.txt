Bank of America near cutting deal for more federal aid: WSJ Bank of America Corp. (BAC) is near cutting a deal with U.S. officials to receive billions more in federal aid, The Wall Street Journal reported late Thursday on its Web site. The agreement will provide $15 billion to $20 billion in fresh capital and backstop $115 billion to $120 billion of bank assets, according to the Journal. Bank of America has already has received $25 billion in federal aid.
