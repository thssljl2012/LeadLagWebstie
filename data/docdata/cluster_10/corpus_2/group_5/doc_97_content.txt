Bank and Broker Default Risk Bespoke Investment Group December 10, 2008 Below we highlight the current CDS prices of 13 global banks and brokers. These prices represent the cost per year to insure $10,000 worth of debt for 5 years. As shown, Morgan Stanley (MS) still has the highest default risk, followed by Goldman (GS), Citigroup (C), Merrill Lynch (MER), UBS (UBS), and Bank of America (BAC). While none of the firms below have CDS less than 100 basis points, HSBC, Deutsche Bank (DB), Wells Fargo (WFC), and JP Morgan (JPM) have the lowest default risk at the moment http://bespokeinvest.typepad.com/bespoke/2008/12/bank-and-broker-default-risk.html
