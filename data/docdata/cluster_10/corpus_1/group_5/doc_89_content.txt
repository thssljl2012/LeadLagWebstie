(Updates Fidelity Investments entry) Dec 12 (Reuters) - Bank of America Corp said on Thursday it plans to eliminate 30,000 to 35,000 jobs over three years, reflecting its pending purchase of Merrill Lynch &amp;amp; Co and weaker business activity. Financial companies worldwide have announced around 290,000 job cuts -- roughly equal to the population of India's capital New Delhi -- since August 2007 when the credit crisis escalated.
