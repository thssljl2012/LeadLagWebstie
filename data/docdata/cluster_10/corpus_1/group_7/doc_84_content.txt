Bank of America Corp. will rebrand its Countrywide Financial Corp. mortgage unit.  According to The Wall Street Journal , Countrywide will be renamed Bank of America Home loans on April 27.  In addition, Charlotte-based BofA (NYSE:BAC) plans to hire 1,000 people in its mortgage unit and move 500 employees to mortgage processing from home-equity processing.  In other developments, BofAs stock has fallen nearly 11 percent in Thursday trading. The companys stock, which has traded between $3.77 and $43.50 per share over the last year, was trading at $4.07 per share in mid-afternoon trading. BofAs stock closed at $4.57 per share on Wednesday.
