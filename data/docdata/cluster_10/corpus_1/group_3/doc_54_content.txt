Diversified global infrastructure, finance and media company General Electric Company (NYSE:GE) announced on Thursday (2 October) that the company has priced its offering of 547,825,000 shares of its common stock at USD22.25 per share. GE said that if the underwriters sell over 547,825,000 shares of common stock, the underwriters have the option to purchase an additional 82,173,750 shares. Banc of America Securities LLC, Citi, Deutsche Bank Securities, Goldman, Sachs &amp;amp; Co, J.P. Morgan and Morgan Stanley are acting as book running lead managers for the offering. Barclays Capital, Credit Suisse and UBS Investment Bank are acting as junior book runners.
