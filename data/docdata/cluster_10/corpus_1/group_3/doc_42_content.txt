Barclays has moved quickly in digesting the U.S. capital markets operations of Lehman Brothers. Now, after having emblazoned its logo on the New York firms headquarters, Barclays has now stamped it on its equity trading and research business.Barclays Capital on Tuesday officially relaunched Lehmans U.S. research and sales business under its imprimatur, the sky blue that is Barclays signature hue replacing the familiar Lehman green. The move follows the integrations of other Barclays-Lehman investment banking activities. Barclays Capital is pleased to re-launch our US Equity operations, Gerald Donini, the former Lehman executive who is now Barclayss head of equities, said in a statement. We will continue to focus on delivering superior equity capital markets content, service, and execution to our clients.
