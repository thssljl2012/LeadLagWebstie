Buffett: $700 billion bailout may not be be big enough - Oct. 2, 2008: "'It will cost more to solve this problem today than it did two weeks ago,' said Buffett, referring to when Treasury Secretary Henry Paulson's first proposed that Congress help rescue Wall Street after Lehman Brothers went bankrupt, Merrill Lynch was sold to Bank of America, and American International Group had to be rescued.'If we don't get it solved next week,' added Buffett, 'I may go back to delivering papers.'"
