 The FTSE 100 Index dived to six-year lows this week as concerns returned to the insurance and banking sectors.HSBC's news of a 62% fall in profits and a 12.5bn rights issue hit banks hard, while record losses for US insurance giant AIG also marked the start of another sell-off for insurers.  The Footsie fell 8% over Monday and Tuesday. It ended the week nearly 300 points down at 3530.7.
