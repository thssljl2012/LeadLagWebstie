Morgan Stanleys moves towards a deal follow a turbulent week in the global financial markets, which began with the collapse of Lehman Brothers. Since then, Barclays has moved to snap up some of Lehman Brothers assets, and in the UK Lloyds TSB has agreed to buy HBOS.
