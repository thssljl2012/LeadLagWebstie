 The new products marketed as Simplified Life will be launched soon with customer promotions starting in September 2008.The partnership will see Barclays offer both level and decreasing term insurance products as non-advised sales through Barclays's website. Gary Duggan, managing director for consumer lending and insurance for Barclays, said: 'The Norwich Union partnership enables us to expand our protection offering to include life insurance - a product which is essential to many customers. We already have a strong relationship with Norwich Union in relation to our motor, travel and home insurance and we look forward to working with them in this area.' Barclays has signed a deal with Norwich Union for the insurer to provide the bank with life insurance products for its mass market customers.
