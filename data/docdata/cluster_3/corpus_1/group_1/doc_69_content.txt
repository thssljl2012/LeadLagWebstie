Stephen Hester, British Land chief executive; Arthur Ryan, former Prudential Financial of the US chairman of; and John McFarlane, a former Australia and New Zealand Banking Group (ANZ) chief executive, will all join the bank's board as non-executives from the start of October, The Daily Telegraph reports.
