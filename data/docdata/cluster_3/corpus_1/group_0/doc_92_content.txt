 WELLINGTON -(Dow Jones)-  (TEL.NZ) said Thursday its Gen-i unit has chosen not to participate in the tender process to provide Commonwealth Bank of Australia's (CBA.AU) telecommunications services, but plans to strengthen investment in other areas of the market. 'Gen-i is re-shaping for profitable growth, with a focus on managed ICT services for the mid-market, particularly for clients with a trans-Tasman focus, ' the company said in a statement.As a result of its strategy, Gen-i has decided not to participate in CBA's tender process for the provision of telecommunications services across Australia. Gen-i has worked with the bank for eight years.The company intends to strengthen its investment in people and capabilities in Australia.
