 LONDON (Reuters) - China Development Bank is proceeding with its planned investment in Barclays (BARC.L: Quote, Profile, Research) as agreed last month, the bank said on Wednesday, after a report China had rejected the 136 million pound deal.Barclays declined to comment further. A person familiar with the matter said CDB's investment was in Hong Kong ready for delivery.Caijing Magazine had reported that the investment by CDB had been rejected by the State Council, or cabinet. The investment is part of a 4.5 billion pound fundraising by Barclays and will maintain CDB's stake at about 3.1 percent.
