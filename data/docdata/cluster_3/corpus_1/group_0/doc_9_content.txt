 New York, July 8 (Reuters) - The following financial services industry appointments were announced on Tuesday. To inform us of other job changes, please e-mail moves@thomsonreuters.com. Wachovia Wealth Management, a unit of Wachovia Corp (WB.N: Quote, Profile, Research, Stock Buzz) hired Dr. Susan Massenzio, a psychologist with two decades of experience in the financial services industry, as associate director of its Family Dynamics unit, where she will work with wealthy investors. The consulting firm on Tuesday announced that Jeff Dodson has joined the company as a partner in its international corporate services practice and will be based in the Dallas office. Prior to KPMG, Dodson was director of tax planning at a chemical company. The private equity firm has appointed David Balint to be director of its Asia Leveraged Finance Group, based out of Sydney, Australia. Previously a director in the Leveraged and Acquisition unit of Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Limited, he has taken up his duties.
