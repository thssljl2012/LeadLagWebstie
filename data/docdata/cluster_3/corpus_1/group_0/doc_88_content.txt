 LONDON, July 27 (Reuters) - Reinsurance giant Swiss Re  is in talks to buy the life assurance arm of British bank Barclays , The Sunday Times newspaper said.The report said Swiss Re was conducting due diligence on the division, which has more than 7 billion pounds ($13.89 billion) under management but closed to new business in 2001.Barclays declined to comment.
