 Bankwest has become the second major bank to lift home loan rates in less than a week.Bankwest has increased its standard variable home loan rate by 0.2 per cent, taking its variable home loan rate to 9.55 per cent.A spokesman for Bankwest says the move follows the higher cost of funds following the global credit crunch.Last week, St George Bank announced mortgage rates would increase by 20 basis points. The Bankwest rate increase also applies to the discount service rate.
