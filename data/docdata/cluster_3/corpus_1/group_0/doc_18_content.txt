 By Hamish Champ Troubled group's bankers seek help in evaluating the business Regent Inns' banks have appointed BDO Stoy Hayward to look at the business.ThePublican.com understands the firm has been brought in by the troubled group, which operates the Walkabout bar chain and Old Orleans restaurant business, to evaluate the company's options going forward.BDO has not been tasked with the job of finding a buyer for Regent at this stage, it is believed.Sunday newspaper reports suggested Regent's banks, which include RBS, Barclays and HSBC, were set to bring in the firm.Regent Inns has seen a number of merger efforts collapse in recent months and at its latest trading update reported that it was unlikely to make a profit in the current financial year.
