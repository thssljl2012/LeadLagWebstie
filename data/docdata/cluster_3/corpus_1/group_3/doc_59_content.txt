The government is to inject up to 37bn of taxpayer cash into Royal Bank of Scotland (RBS) Lloyds TSB and HBOS.RBS is to raise 20bn, with chief executive Sir Fred Goodwin quitting the firm after his bank was forced to go to the Treasury for the bail-out.A further 17bn will be put into HBOS and Lloyds TSB.
