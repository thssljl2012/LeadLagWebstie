 Westpac has announced it is increasing its interest rates, making it the last of the major banks to announce a rate rise.From Thursday, the bank will lift its standard variable home loan rate by 0.14 of a per cent to 9.61 per cent.Westpac's increase is equivalent to the Commonwealth Bank's, and slightly below ANZ and National Australia Bank's.Other smaller lenders including St George and Bankwest have also lifted rates, as they try to recoup the higher costs of funding their loans.
