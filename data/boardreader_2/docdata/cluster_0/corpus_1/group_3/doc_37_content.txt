The passage of the financial sector bailout plan through the US Senate has failed to boost Australian stocks.By the close of trade today, the ASX 200 had fallen 34 points to 4,761 and the All Ordinaries index was down 0.8 of one per cent to 4,774.The banks were mixed, with the Commonwealth Bank gaining more than 1 per cent while the ANZ lost nearly 2 per cent.The miners were weaker, with Rio Tinto losing 4 per cent after surging 12 per cent yesterday.At about 4:50pm AEST the Australian dollar was trading at 79.01 US cents.
