US shares went into a new freefall overnight after the Federal Reserve's massive bailout of insurance giant AIG failed to calm a financial market storm and investors rushed for safe haven assets. The Dow Jones Industrial Average slid 449 points, or 4.06 percent, to end at 10,609. The Nasdaq composite nose-dived 109 points, or 4.94 percent, to 2,098 and the broad-market Standard &amp;amp; Poor's 500 index lost 57 points, or 4.71 percent, to 1,156.
