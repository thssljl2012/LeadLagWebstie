LONDON -- Investors continued to fret about the health of the global financial sector on Tuesday after insurance giant AIG was downgraded by ratings agency Standard &amp;amp; Poor's and Aegon and Natixis revealed they have exposure to failed U.S. investment bank Lehman Brothers . Shares in Aegon fell 2.2%, while Natixis shares dropped 4%. Barclays shares dropped 4.8% after the bank said it's interested in buying some Lehman assets. Overall, the U.K. FTSE 100 index fell 1.5% to 5,126.20, the German DAX 30 index lost 1.6% to 5,967.61 and the French CAC-40 index fell 1.5% to 4,108.04.Copyright
