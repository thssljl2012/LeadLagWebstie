Financial firms Citigroup Inc. (NYSE:C) and Bank of America suffered losses, by 8.19 percent and 9.17 percent, respectively. Ford Motor Co. (NYSE:F), although helped by its position in the proposed $14 billion auto industry bailout debate in Washington, fell 8.57 percent. In London, the FTSE 100 index rose 21.41 to 4,388.69, up 0.49 percent.
