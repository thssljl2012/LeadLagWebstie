(The Australian Financial Review - ABIX via COMTEX) -- The Australian sharemarket posted solid gains on 19 February 2009, with the S&amp;P/ASX 200 rising 35.7 points to close at 3,448.9. The All Ordinaries Index was up 31.1 points at 3,398. The Commonwealth Bank rose $A0.69 to finish at $A29.85 and Sims Metal Management ended the session $A0.66 higher at $A17.27.
