 SYDNEY, Aug 27 (Reuters) - Australia and New Zealand Banking Group ,Australia's third-largest lender, has launched a A$500 million ($427.4 million)issue of convertible preference shares, it said on Wednesday.     The securities will convert into ordinary shares in 2014 and will pay amargin between 250 and 290 basis points over the bank bill swap rate.
