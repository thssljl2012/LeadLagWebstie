  BEIJING (XFN-ASIA) - Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd (ANZ) said it has received approval from the China Banking Regulatory Commission to establish a branch in Guangzhou. ANZ said it will start preparation work to set up the branch, which will be its third in China. The bank has had a representative office in Guangzhou since 2006.
