 Data from InfoChoice indicate that a number of Australian banks have reduced the interest rates on their term deposit products. The ANZ Bank, Westpac, National Australia Bank and Macquarie Bank are among the banking groups to cut their term deposit rates, by amounts ranging from 0.25 per cent to 0.40 per cent. Meanwhile, the four major banks have reduced the interest rates on their fixed-rate home loans by 0.20 per cent to 0.50 per cent, as a result of lower short-term funding costs
