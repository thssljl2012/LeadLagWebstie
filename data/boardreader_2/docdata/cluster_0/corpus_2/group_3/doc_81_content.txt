(CEP News) - The Australian dollar was routed in currency markets Monday, falling nearly 10% against the U.S. dollar ahead of an anticipated rate cut.     The Reserve Bank of Australia is expected to lower interest rates by 50 basis points to 6.50% at 11:30 p.m. EDT.     The Australian dollar is down 0.0720, or 9.28%, to 0.7021 against the U.S. dollar, and lower by 11.000, or 13.4%, to 70.475 against the Japanese yen.
