Gold Fields has been upgraded from underperform to buy by Bank of America-Merrill Lynch, which cited a 'positive outlook on the gold price'. The brokerage raised its price estimate for the stock by 36 percent to R125. 'Gold's allure as a safe haven will likely support investor demand going forward,' the brokerage wrote in a report on Thursday. Shares rose 0.1 percent to close at R103.70, while the gold mining sector fell 1.96 percent. - Bloomberg
