Stocks fluctuate as investors battle uncertainty Monday March 9, 11:55 am ET By Sara Lepro, AP Business Writer Stocks fluctuate as investors battle economic uncertainty; Bank of America report boosts banks NEW YORK (AP) -- Wall Street is struggling to find a direction as investors vacillate between hopes for the financial sector and pessimism about the broader economy's slide. Financial stocks are higher on a report that Bank of America could raise capital in the private sector. Analysts see this as a positive alternative to the government buying bigger stakes in banks. Meanwhile, drugmakers Merck and Schering-Plough are combining in a $41 billion deal. However, layoffs at McClatchy Co. are weighing on the market. In midday trading, the Dow Jones industrial average is down 2 points at 6,624. The Standard &amp; Poor's 500 index is up 2 at 685, while the Nasdaq composite index is down 3 at 1,290.
