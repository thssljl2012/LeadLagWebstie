Two brothers have been remanded in custody accused of murdering a banker who died after confronting a gang.Tom and Ben Cowles, aged 21 and 20, of Costessey, faced Norwich magistrates charged with killing Barclays Wealth boss Frank McGarahan, 45, in the city.Ben Cowles is also charged with three counts of assault - one against a Lithuanian man and offences against Mr McGarahan's brother and cousin.
