charged over banker's murderTodayTwo men have been charged with the murder of a banker who was set upon after confronting a gang in a city centre. Tom and Ben Cowles, aged 21 and 20, of Costessey, Norfolk, will appear in court on Friday accused of killing Frank McGarahan, 45, following an incident in Norwich in the early hours of Sunday.
