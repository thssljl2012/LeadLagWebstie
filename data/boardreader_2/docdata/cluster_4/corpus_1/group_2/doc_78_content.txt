A six-month pregnant bank worker was punched in the face during an armed robbery.The 19-year-old woman was assaulted as three balaclava-clad men struck at Barclays Bank in Anchorage Quay at about 5pm on Tuesday.They threatened members of staff inside the branch as they demanded the cash and the pregnant worker was grabbed by the arm and attacked.Police said the men were armed with a shotgun and a hammer.No shots were fire and no-one was seriously injured as the raiders left empty-handed and made off in a dark blue Mercedes, which was later found abandoned near the Broadway Pub in Broadway.
