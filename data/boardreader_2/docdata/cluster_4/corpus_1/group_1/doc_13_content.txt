 7:43 PM ET PARAMUS, N.J. (AP) - Vijay Singh (sihng) has won The Barclays for a record fourth time. He defeated Sergio Garcia with a birdie on the second extra hole.The 45-year-old Fijian closed with a 1-under 70 to match Garcia and Kevin Sutherland at 8-under 276 on the sun-baked Ridgewood Country Club course in suburban New Jersey. This was the first time the event was held there.Singh won in 1993, 1995 and 2006 at Westchester Country Club. This was his 33rd PGA Tour victory. He takes the lead after the first event of the PGA Tour's playoff format.
