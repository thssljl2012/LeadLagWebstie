 PARAMUS, N.J. - Steve Stricker made a spectacular eagle on his way to a 7-under 64 to seize a three-shot lead after the second round of the PGA Barclays Classic on Friday.Stricker also registered five birdies for a 10-under 132 total.Stricker's eagle came on the third hole when his third shot from 70 yards flew past the hole before spinning back into the cup.Hunter Mahan followed his opening 62 with a 73 and is in second place at 7-under 135 in the first event of the PGA Tour's FedExCup playoffs.'That's golf,' said Mahan. 'Some days are easy and some days are hard. This is a hard day.'Tied for third at 136 are Kenny Perry, the FedExCup points leader in the absence of the injured Tiger Woods, Dudley Hart (69) and 2007 U.S. Open champion Angel Cabrera.Angela Park is alone at the top of the leaderboard after shooting a 6-under 66 in the first round of the Safeway Classic at Columbia Edgewater Country Club in Portland.Park is in search of her first career LPGA title in her second year on tour.
