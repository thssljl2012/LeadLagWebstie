 A man who looked like the robber was seen making a bet earlier in the dayAn e-fit image of a man police want to speak to about a bank robbery in Sussex has been released by police.A branch of Barclays Bank on High Street, Henfield, was robbed on Tuesday, 19 August. Sussex Police said a man who matches the description of the armed robber was seen earlier in the day. They want to speak to a man who was seen placing a bet on a dog race at the Betfred bookmakers in Henfield about two hours before the robbery.
