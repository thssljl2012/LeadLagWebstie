Item 1. Summary Term Sheet The information set forth in the Offer to Purchase is hereby amended and supplemented by adding the following language thereto: The Company hereby terminates the Tender Offer pursuant to the conditions specified in the Offer to Purchase, which provide (among other things) that the Company may terminate the Tender Offer if, on or prior to the Expiration Date, the National Master Freight Agreement Amendment Condition shall not have been satisfied. The National Master Freight Agreement Amendment Condition means that employees of subsidiaries of the Company who are subject to the National Master Freight Agreement, effective April 1, 2008 through March 31, 2013, between the International Brotherhood of Teamsters and Trucking Management Inc., a multi-employer representative for certain subsidiaries of the Company, shall have ratified an amendment to that agreement regarding a wage reduction. As of 12:00 midnight, New York City time, on December 23, 2008 (the Expiration Date), the National Master Freight Agreement Amendment Condition was not satisfied. As a result, the Company will not accept for purchase, or pay for, Notes tendered pursuant to the Tender Offer. Any Notes tendered in connection with the Tender Offer will be returned promptly to the tendering Holders by the Depositary. Item 4.
