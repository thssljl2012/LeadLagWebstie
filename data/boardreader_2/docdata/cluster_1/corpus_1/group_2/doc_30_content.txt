SALEM  State officials are warning policy holders of AIG insurance to be careful if approached to replace their policy.The subsidiary of AIG that issues insurance in Oregon is state-regulated and has plenty of assets to pay claims, state officials said. AIG insurance companies are financially sound, so its policies are not in jeopardy.The state Insurance Division plans to remind insurance companies and agents not to make misleading statements about the financial condition of AIG.For more information, go to www.insurance.oregon.gov or call (888) 877-4894.
