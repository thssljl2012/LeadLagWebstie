Affiliates of AIG American International Group Inc. collected $23.9 billion in premiums last year statewide, according to the Missouri Department of Insurance.Those 27 AIG units paid out a combined $17.2 billion in claims, losses and surrender value of policies, the department said. This is a holding company problem, said Linda Bohrer, acting director of the Missouri Department of Insurance. The (AIG) insurance company businesses governed by the states are financially sound. The individual insurance companies have adequate capital and the policyholders interests are a high priority at every level at AIG, the Federal Reserve and with state regulators, Bohrer said.
