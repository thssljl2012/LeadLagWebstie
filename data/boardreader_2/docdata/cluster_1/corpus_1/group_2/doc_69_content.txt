SAO PAULO -(Dow Jones)- American International Group Inc. (AIG) is mulling the sale of its Brazilian assets to help it handle its financial difficulties, Brazilian daily newspaper Folha de Sao Paulo reported in its Thursday edition, without citing sources.AIG has had a partnership with local bank Unibanco (UBB) since 1997 in which the two companies provide insurance and pension fund services.In addition, AIG has a 7.34% stake in Providencia Industria e Comercio SA ( PRVI3.BR), a Brazilian manufacturer of plastics and textiles.
