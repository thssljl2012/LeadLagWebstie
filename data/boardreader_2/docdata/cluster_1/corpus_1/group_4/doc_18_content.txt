TAIPEI -(Dow Jones)- American International Group Inc. (AIG) plans to sell part of its 95% stake in Taiwanese life-insurance unit Nan Shan Life Insurance Co., a Nan Shan Life official said Monday.  AIG wants to retain management control over Nan Shan Life and isn't planning to sell its entire stake as reported in the Economic Daily News on Monday, said the official, who declined to be named.  'This was what I was briefed in October. From what I understand, there's been nothing to indicate otherwise,' the official said.  She declined to elaborate.
