NEW DELHI -(Dow Jones)- The Indian unit of American International Group Inc. ( AIG) will continue its insurance joint ventures with the Tata Group and run its asset management company in the current form, its country head said Monday.  'Its business as usual. Both the companies are functioning the way they were,' Sunil Mehta told reporters on the sidelines of the India Economic Summit.  AIG runs Tata AIG Life Insurance Co. and Tata AIG General Insurance Co. with the Tata Group.
