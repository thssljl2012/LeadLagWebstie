American International Group, (News/Aktienkurs) Inc. (AIG) announced today that certain of its domestic life insurance subsidiaries have entered into a securities lending agreement with the Federal Reserve Bank of New York. (News) Under the Securities Lending Agreement, the New York Fed will borrow, on an overnight basis, investment grade fixed income securities from these AIG subsidiaries in return for cash collateral. As expected, drawdowns under the existing New York Fed credit facility have been used, in part, to settle securities lending transactions.
