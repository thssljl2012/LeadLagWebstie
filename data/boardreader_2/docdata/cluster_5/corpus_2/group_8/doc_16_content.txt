  Aussie Pride! , International Cricket Captain:Ashes 2006 Edition  by Lonie_from_50           Commonwealth Bank Series - Australia vs India at Adelaide       20 January 2008   India get back at Aussies with good win      A good decision to win the toss and bat by Rahul Dravid has seen the Indians take out victory against the Australians for the second time in the Commonwealth Bank Series. Dravid won the toss and elected to bowl, and Joginder Sharma and co did the damage, taking control early on.    Matthew Hayden top scored, while skipper Dravid got the winning runs in a good batting display, making 72 not out, while Laxman stood with him making an unbeaten 40. Mitchell Johnson took 2 wickets to be the best bowler for Australia.     Toss:  India   Result:  India win by 7 wickets   Man of Match:  R Dravid (India)     Australia:  M Hayden, +A Gilchrist, *R Ponting, M Clarke, A Symonds, M Hussey, D Thornely, C White, M Johnson, N Bracken, B Hilfenhaus, S Watson (12th Man)     AUSTRALIA - 165 (37.2 ov)   M Hayden 32 (28)  J Sharma 3-43     INDIA - 166 for 3 (39.3 ov)   R Dravid 72* (108)  V Laxman 40 (69)  M Johnson 2-31
