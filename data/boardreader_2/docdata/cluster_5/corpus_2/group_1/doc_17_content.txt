 Bangladesh in Australia, 2008    Bangladesh sink to new low     30 August 2008   Bangladesh have suffered one of their heaviest losses in the ODI history in the opening match of the three match Commonwealth Bank series against Australia at Marrara cricket ground in Darwin today. Bangladesh could only manage 74 runs before getting all out in just 27.4 overs in response to 254 for 8 posted by Australia.
