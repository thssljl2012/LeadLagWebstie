Total commercial finance fell 10.4 per cent in November, seasonally adjusted, to $28.375 billion, from $31.669 billion in October.  Lease finance fell 3.0 per cent in November to $518 million, compared with $535 million in October.  Housing finance for owner occupation rose 1.4 per cent to $12.460 billion in November from $12.290 billion in October.
