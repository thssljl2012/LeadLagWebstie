 Market watch top headlines Australian reports Aust markets:  :  :  World reports World commodities:  World markets:   RFG, UGL, COU, MOC, CFX, ALK, NEW, ADI, CBA, CAS, PNA, ILU,   AAP 2009-01-19   Merchandise imports into Australia fell 2.0 per cent in December in seasonally adjusted terms.  In unadjusted terms, merchandise imports fell by $772 million, to $20.130 billion, from a downwardly revised $20.902 billion in November, the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) said on Monday.  Imports of mineral fuels, lubricants and related materials fell to $2.291 billion in December from $2.571 billion a month earlier, while imports of machinery and transport equipment fell to $7.450 billion, from $7.833 billion.
