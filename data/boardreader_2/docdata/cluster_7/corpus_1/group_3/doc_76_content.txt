The fund was valued at $A63.4bn at the end of the quarter, compared with $A64.2bn three months earlier. Meanwhile, the fund has increased its exposure to global bank debt and the Australian mortgage market, while it has invested around $A3bn in Australian and international banks. The fund's portfolio includes a 9.7 per cent weighting toward Australian shares, while 20 per cent of its funds under management are invested in international shares.
