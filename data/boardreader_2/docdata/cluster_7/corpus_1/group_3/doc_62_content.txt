SYDNEY, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Australia and New Zealand Banking Group , Australia's third largest lender, is cutting its variable mortgage rate by 0.25 percent, the bank said on Friday.Effective from Monday Oct 27, the interest rate would be cut to 8.32 percent, ANZ said.ANZ and its largest Australian competitors, cut variable rates by 0.80 percentage points earlier this month, after the Reserve Bank of Australia cut the official cash rate by a surprisingly large 1 percent to 6.0 percent.
