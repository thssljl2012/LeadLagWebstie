SYDNEY, Dec 24 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd , the nation's top lender, has ended talks to buy   non-bank lender Wizard after its offer was not accepted, NAB said   in a statement on Wednesday.   Earlier, Commonwealth Bank of Australia Ltd said it   will buy up to A$4 billion mortgages originated by Wizard, a unit   of General Electric Co.
