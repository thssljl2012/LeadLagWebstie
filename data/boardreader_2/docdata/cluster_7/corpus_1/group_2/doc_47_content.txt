MELBOURNE, Sept 16, 2008 (Asia In Focus via COMTEX News Network) --AUSTRALIA AND NEW ZEALAND BANKING GROUP LTD (ANZ) (ASX:ANZ) says its total exposure to the LEHMAN group of companies is around US$120 million (A$148.5 million). The exposure comprises a US$28 million exposure to LEHMAN BROTHERS HOLDINGS INC and US$92 million to Lehman subsidiaries, the bank said in a statement.* 'ANZ is not in a position at this time to provide an estimate of the likely loss, if any,' it said.* ANZ added that Lehman subsidiaries are not part of the parent's Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing in the US yesterday.(C) Copyright 2008 Asia In Focus
