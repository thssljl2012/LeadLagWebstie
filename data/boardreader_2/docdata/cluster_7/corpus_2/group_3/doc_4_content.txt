CBA have joined the party...       http://news.theage.com.au/business/c...1020-54mf.html       Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA) has dropped its interest rates on both variable and fixed home loans after its rivals National Australia Bank, ANZ Banking Group and Aussie Home Loans all reduced their rates over the weekend.      CBA will reduce its standard variable rate home loan by 21 basis points to 8.32 per cent, and its basic variable home loan to 7.81 per cent, the bank said in a statement.      The new rates apply to both new and existing customers and will take effect from Thursday October 30.
