SYDNEY -(Dow Jones)- Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd. (ANZ) said Friday it has reached an agreement with the liquidators of Opes Prime and that the principle settlement amount is about A$253 million in cash and other assets.  That's the global settlement amount for all parties involved.  ANZ said in a statement this represents a return of 80 cents on the dollar to close positions.   -  Cynthia Koons  , Dow Jones Newswires; 61-3-8272-4691; cynthia.koons@ dowjones.com   (END) Dow Jones Newswires 03-05-092057ET Copyright (c) 2009 Dow Jones &amp; Company, Inc.
