In this role, Mr Brown will have responsibility for all treasury activities, including funding and balance sheet management. He will report to GMAC chief risk officer Sam Ramsey.  Prior to joining GMAC, Mr Brown spent more than 10 years at Bank of America, most recently serving as corporate treasurer. Prior to his role as corporate treasurer, he worked in a number of other capacities at Bank of America. Mr Ramsey said: 'Jeff's experience and leadership will be a valuable asset to the organization, as we build our business and enhance our treasury function.'  US-based GMAC Financial Services has appointed Jeffrey Brown as its corporate treasurer.
