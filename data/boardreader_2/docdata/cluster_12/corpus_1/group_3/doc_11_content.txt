Goldman Sachs led a $12 million second venture funding round for Nimsoft Inc.The Redwood City business, led by CEO Gary Read, sells software used in monitoring IT infrastructure. Its 600 customers include Barclays Capital, CB Richard Ellis, Fusion Storm, KPMG, Northrop Grumman and Wells Fargo (NYSE: WFC). Nimsoft brought in $35 million in revenue in 2007.Goldman Sachs (NYSE: GS) put David Campbell on Nimsofts board of directors as part of the financing.First round investors JMI Equity and Northzone Ventures also gave money this time.Peter Arrowsmith of JMI and Torleif Ahlsand of Northzone both already work on the board.
