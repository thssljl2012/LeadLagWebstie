Dec 9 (Reuters) - E.I. du Pont de Nemours and Company   (DuPont) on Tuesday sold $1 billion of five-year notes,   according to IFR, a Thomson Reuters publication.   Banc of America Securities, Credit Suisse and Goldman Sachs   were the active bookrunning managers for the sale. JP Morgan,   Morgan Stanley, RBS Greenwich Capital and UBS Investment Bank   were the passive bookrunning managers for the sale.   BORROWER: E.I.
