Commonwealth Bank of Australia will purchase up to A$4 billion of mortgages from Wizard, a non-bank lender owned by General Electric.  According to The Australian, Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA) will buy A$2 billion of mortgages at the end of February 2009. Talks pertaining to purchase of the additional A$2 billion of mortgages are still on.  CBA did not disclose the acquisition price, said Reuters. National Australia Bank was the other company in fray to acquire the mortgages from Wizard. However, it ended talks with Wizard after its offer was rejected.
