SYDNEY -(Dow Jones)- Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd. (ANZ) said Monday it may issue a U.S. dollar denominated, Australia government guaranteed bond later Monday. 'Depending on market conditions, we will be looking to execute a deal as soon as today,' a spokesperson said. Following a roadshow in the U.S. last week, the bank received a 'favorable reaction' to the possibility of a guaranteed debt issue, the spokesperson said. -By Enda Curran, Dow Jones Newswires; 61-2-8235-2958; enda.curran@dowjones.com
