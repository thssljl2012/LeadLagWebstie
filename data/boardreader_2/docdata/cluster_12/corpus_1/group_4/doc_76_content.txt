Article from: AAP  Font size: Decrease Increase  : Email  : Print  TWO funds of Allco Financial Group, which went into voluntary administration this month, have been granted extentions to the suspension of trading in their securities.  Allco's property fund, Record Realty, and the investment firm's hybrid security fund, Allco Hybrid Investment Trust, requested the continued suspension of securities because they cannot provide further information to the market, the trusts said in statements.  Both securities have been in trading halt since November 7, the day National Australia Bank appointed receivers and mangers to Allco unit Rubicon Holdings.  The funds will update the market before trading begins on November 28.
