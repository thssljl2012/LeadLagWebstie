(The Sydney Morning Herald - ABIX via COMTEX) -- CBAUF   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating -- Allco Finance has indicated it may have to appoint a voluntary administrator without the express support of its banking syndicate. The Australian financial services group has yet to secure the backing of all members to roll over payments totalling $A147.5m, due in the last two months of 2008. Local banks Westpac Banking and Commonwealth Bank of Australia are willing to give Allco more time, but overseas members of the syndicate are reportedly more reluctant. Allco says it will not divest assets at prices that are too low, despite pressure to reduce its debt.
