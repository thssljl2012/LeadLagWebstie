Mr McDonald was appointed a non-executive director of National Australia Bank Limited in December 2005.  Mr McDonald joined the Group as a director of Bank of New Zealand in August 1991 and was appointed Chairman in 1997. Mr McDonald has retired as Chairman of Bank of New Zealand.  Chairman of National Australia Bank Limited, Mr Michael Chaney thanked Mr McDonald for his significant contribution to the Board and the Bank of New Zealand during his 17 year association with the National Australia Bank Group.
