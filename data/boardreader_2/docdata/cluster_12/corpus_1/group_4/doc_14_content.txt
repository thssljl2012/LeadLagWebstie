SYDNEY, Nov 12 (Reuters) - Australia and New Zealand Banking Group  has appointed Rod Eddington as new chairman, Australia's third-largest lender said on Wednesday.  Eddington would replace Charles Goode and join the board early in the second half of 2009.  Four new directors would be appointed to the ANZ Board over the next 12 months, the bank also said. (Reporting by Mette Fraende)
