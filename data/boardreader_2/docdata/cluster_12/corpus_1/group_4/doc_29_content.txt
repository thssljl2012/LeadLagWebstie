Westpac Banking Corp today confirmed it had an exposure to failed Allco Finance Group (Allco) of approximately $200 million.  Westpac will review the implications of the appointment of the voluntary administrators to Allco and adjust existing provisions as required, the bank said in a statement after the market closed.  Allco was placed in voluntary administration last night by its syndicate of 12 domestic and offshore banks, including Commonwealth Bank of Australia, National Australia Bank, Westpac, and St George Bank.
