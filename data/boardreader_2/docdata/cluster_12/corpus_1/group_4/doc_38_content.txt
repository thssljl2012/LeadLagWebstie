Commonwealth Bank of Australia Ltd (CBA) has signed a four-year agreement to provide online share trading and equities portfolio administration services with Westpac Banking Corp.  The agreement is between Westpac subsidiaries Westpac Securities Ltd and BT Portfolio Services Ltd and CBA subsidiary Australian Investment Exchange Ltd, which is part of its brokerage arm, CommSec.  CommSec managing director Matt Comyn said CBA was committed to becoming the leading provider of wholesale financial services in Australia.
