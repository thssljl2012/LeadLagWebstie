(0939.HK) shares in a placement, trimming its stake in the Chinese lender to 16.6% from 19.13%, a person familiar with the situation said Wednesday.The U.S. lender sold the shares at a fixed price of HK$3.92 a share, or a discount of 12% to China Construction Bank's closing price Tuesday of HK$4.45, the person said.The person said the placement was oversubscribed, and investors alloted the shares are subject to a lockup period of 120 days.UBS AG (UBS) and Merrill Lynch &amp;amp; Co.
