.In accordance with the terms of a Eurobond issue taken out in 1998, the Bank for Development and Foreign Economic Affairs (Vnesheconombank) has paid nearly $190.652m to Citibank N.A. at the Russian Finance Ministry's request, the bank said in a statement today. The transfer was made in a single currency to pay off the interest on the bond loan.  The Eurobond issue worth some $3.466bn was placed with a maturity period of 20 years, with coupon payments to be made every six months.
