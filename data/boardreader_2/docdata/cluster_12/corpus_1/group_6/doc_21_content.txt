The China Construction Bank said on Thursday its strategic cooperation with the Bank of America will not be affected by the US lender's sale of a 2.4 percent stake in the Chinese bank. Bank of America is still the CCB's second largest shareholder. The Bank of America sold 5.62 billion CCB shares for 2.8 billion US dollars on Wednesday, to cope with a cash strain due to the global financial crisis.  The China Construction Bank said its strategic cooperation  with the Bank of America will not be affected.
