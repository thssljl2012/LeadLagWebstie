Jan 15 (Reuters) - Metropolitan Edison Co, a unit of FirstEnergy Corp, on Wednesday sold $300 million in 10-year senior notes, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters service.  Bank of America, Goldman Sachs, and Scotiabank were the joint bookrunning managers for the sale.
