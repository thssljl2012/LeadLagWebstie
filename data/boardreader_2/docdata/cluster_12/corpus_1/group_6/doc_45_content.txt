Jan 21 (Reuters) - Electricite de France SA on  Wednesday sold $5.0 billion in three parts in the 144a private  placement market, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters service.  Bank of America, Barclays Capital, BNP Paribas, and HSBC  Securities were the joint bookrunning managers for the sale.
