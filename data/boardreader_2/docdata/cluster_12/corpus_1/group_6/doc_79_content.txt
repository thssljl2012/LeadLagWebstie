SYDNEY, Jan 7 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank has added A$500 million ($361.5 million) to its five-year government-guaranteed bond, priced at around 110 basis points over swap, two investors said on Wednesday.  The private placement was led by UBS. UBS declined to comment.  Commonwealth Bank and Suncorp-Metway are also eyeing further issuance of government-backed bonds in offshore markets, market sources said.
