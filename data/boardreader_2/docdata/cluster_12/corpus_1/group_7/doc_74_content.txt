The Commonwealth Bank of Australia has posted a cash profit of $A2.01bn for the first half of 2008-09, which is 16 per cent lower than previously. The banking major increased its provision for bad debts from $A333m previously to $A1.6bn, while shareholders will receive an interim dividend of $A1.13 per share. Meanwhile, CEO Ralph Norris believes the Australian economy will not go into recession.
