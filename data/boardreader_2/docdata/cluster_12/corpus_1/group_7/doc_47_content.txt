Feb 12 (Reuters) - Independent oil and gas company Forest Oil Corp late on Wednesday sold $600 million of five-year senior notes in the 144a private placement market, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters service.  The size of the deal was increased from an originally planned $350 million.  JP Morgan, Bank of America, Credit Suisse, Deutsche Bank and Wachovia were the joint bookrunning managers for the sale.
