Frances Total is planning a five-year benchmark euro bond according to reports.   Guidance has been set at mid-swaps plus 87 to 90 basis points, said IFR Markets, a Thomson Reuters online news and market analysis service, citing market sources.   Total has appointed Calyon and Bank of America/Merrill Lynch to manage the issue, IFR said.   Total is rated double A by Standard &amp; Poor's and Fitch Ratings and Aa1 by Moody's Investors Service.
