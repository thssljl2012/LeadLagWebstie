City Bank (NASDAQ: CTBK), a financial institution headquartered in Lynnwood, Washington, said on 13 February that at a meeting held on 11 February 2009 its board of directors had voted to suspend the payment of quarterly cash dividends on the bank's common stock with immediate effect. The board decided that a suspension of dividend payouts would help the bank maintain strong capital and cash reserves during the current economic downturn. According to City Bank the board believes that this is a prudent step to take and is ultimately in the best long-term interest of shareholders.
