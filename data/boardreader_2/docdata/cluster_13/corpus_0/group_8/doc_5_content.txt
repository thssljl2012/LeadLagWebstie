Bomb explodes outside Citibank branch in Athens                                                        By MarketWatch        Last update: 12:35 a.m. EDT March 9, 2009        Comments: 2                                                   ATHENS (AFP) -- A bomb exploded outside a Citibank branch north ofAthens early Monday, causing significant damage but no apparentinjuries, police said.                                The bomb had been placed behind the bank in the suburb of Psychiko. Twocars were also damaged in the blast, a police source said.
