TOPEKA, KS--(Marketwire - March 2, 2009) -  Collective Brands, Inc. (NYSE: PSS) today announced that the Company will present at two New York-based investment community conferences this month -- the Bank of America Consumer ("B of A") Conference and the Telsey Advisory Group ("TAG") Conference.  The Company will present at the B of A Conference on Thursday, March 12, at 1:40 p.m. ET. Matt Rubel, Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President, will present.  The conference will take place at the Palace Hotel.
