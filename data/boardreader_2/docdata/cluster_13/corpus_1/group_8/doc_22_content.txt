ATHENS (AFP)--A bomb exploded outside a Citibank branch north of Athens early Monday, causing significant damage but no apparent injuries, police said.   The bomb had been placed behind the bank in the suburb of Psychiko. Two cars were also damaged in the blast, a police source said.
