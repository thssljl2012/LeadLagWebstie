ATHENS (AFP)--Greek far-left organization Revolutionary Struggle on Wednesday claimed responsibility for two recent bomb attacks on U.S.-based banking group Citibank, state television NET reported.  In a proclamation sent to Greek weekly Pontiki, the group said it exploded a bomb outside a Citibank branch north of Athens early Monday that caused significant damage but no injuries.  Revolutionary Struggle also said it had placed a powerful car bomb outside the bank's Athens headquarters in the district of Kifissia that was defused by police.
