Stonington -- Stonington police have arrested a North Stonington man for allegedly robbing the Citizens Bank branch in Pawcatuck this afternoon.  Police said they arrested Paul Pierce, 51, of 378 Pendleton Hill Road, North Stonington at his home about 8 p.m. He was charged with third-degree robbery and fourth-degree larceny.  He was being held on a $25,000 bond and is scheduled to appear in New London Superior Court this morning.  Police said a man wearing a New England Patriots jacket made off with cash during the 3:24 p.m. robbery. Police said no weapon was shown and there were no injuries.
