   They live in Hurricane alley.  Build a hurricane proof house  or pay the higher rates.        Yes, hurricane resistant houses are a reality, and have been for quite a few years.  They feature pronounced curves, with a uniform outer coating that significantly increases aerodynamics.  What does that do exactly?  It means that the cheap siding you had those day laborers put up doesn't blow off, thus exposing the interior walls to the massive amounts of water hurricanes like to dump.  There's also techniques like using reinforced concrete stilts, instead of wooden ones, for houses in areas prone to the hurricane surge.
