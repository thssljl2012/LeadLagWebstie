UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT MIDDLE DISTRICT OF FLORIDA TAMPA DIVISION SOLOMON TECHNOLOGIES, INC., Plaintiff, vs. TOYOTA MOTOR CORPORATION, TOYOTA MOTOR MANUFACTURING NORTH AMERICA, and TOYOTA MOTOR SALES, U.S.A., Defendants. / CASE NO.: 8:05-cv-01702-T-27MAP JOINT MOTION FOR ENLARGEMENT OF TIME TO FILE JOINT CHART Plaintiff Solomon Technologies, Inc. (Solomon) and Defendants Toyota Motor Corporation, Toyota Motor Manufacturing North America, and Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., (collectively, Toyota), hereby jointly move for a fifteen-day extension of time to file their joint chart pursuant to the Courts Orders of December 11, 2008 (Dkt 49) and January 8, 2009 (Dkt 53). The joint chart is presently due on February 17, 2009. The extension of time would give the parties until March 4, 2009 to file the chart. This request for an extension of time is necessitated by the recent move of Solomons counsel to a new law firm and by the logistics for Toyotas counsel of communicating with their clients in Japan.
