Here's an update/lowdown (get those credit cards and dialing fingers/mouse fingers ready!):          Panther playoff tickets go on sale Thursday   Posted: Tuesday, Dec. 30, 2008         Tickets for the Carolina Panthers' Jan. 10 home playoff game will go on sale 10 a.m. Thursday.         Prices will range from $62 to $400, and each fan can buy up to four tickets. There will be no sales at the Panthers ticket office at Bank of America Stadium. Tickets will be available at Ticketmaster by calling 704-522-6500 in the Charlotte area or going to  www.ticketmaster.com . Details: 704-358-7800 (8:30 a.m.-5:30 p.m. Monday-Friday).       - David Scott
