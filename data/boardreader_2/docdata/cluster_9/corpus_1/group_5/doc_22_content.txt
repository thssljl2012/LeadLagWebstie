Dallas police are looking for a man who robbed a Bank of America branch at a Casa Linda-area grocery store Friday night.   The man entered the Albertson's about 6 p.m. and demanded money from a teller, according to a police report.   The man fled in an unknown direction after the teller gave him $1,000 in cash, the report said.   The man was described as about 35, 6 feet tall and 175 pounds.   He was wearing a hooded sweat shirt, the report said.
