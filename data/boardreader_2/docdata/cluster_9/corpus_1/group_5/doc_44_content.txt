Yakima -- People are coming togther to help Yakima Speedway's groundskeeper after a Friday morning fire destroyed his home.  Co-workers are trying to get Stan Blaine and his wife back on their feet by collecting donations and cleaning up what's left of their home now that the property is a total loss.   Steve Conduff, Yakima Speedway says, '[Stan's wife] is crippled and she can't get around with out a wheelchair, we're working to try and find her some real help as far as getting another wheelchair.'If you want to help they're collecting donations at 1512 Garfield in Yakima. There's also an account at U.S. Bank being opened on Monday under the name Stan Blaine.
