 Police released surveillance photos of a man robbing a Bank of America branch on Monday morning.  The man was wearing a dark ball cap; light-colored, long-sleeved shirt; dark pants; and white sneakers. He's described as 5-foot-10 to 6-foot, and in his late 30s-to early-40s.  He entered the bank at 4300 N. Atlantic Ave. at 9:13 a.m. and handed a teller a note demanding money.  The teller gave him an undisclosed amount, and the suspect fled on foot.  No one was injured.  Anyone with information is asked to contact Cocoa Beach police at 868-3251 or Crimeline at 1-800-423-TIPS.  Contact Spitzer at 242-3722 or mspitzer@floridatoday.com.
