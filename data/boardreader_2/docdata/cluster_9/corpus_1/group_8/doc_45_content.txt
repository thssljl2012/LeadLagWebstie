Shaler police and the FBI are searching for a man who robbed the First Commonwealth Bank at 900 Saxonburg Blvd., this morning.  Police said the man, described as a thin white male, between 20 and 30, about 6 feet tall and clean shaven, was wearing a gray hooded sweatshirt, a ballcap and blue jeans when he entered the bank at about 10 a.m.  He did not show a weapon but passed a note to a teller before fleeing with cash.
