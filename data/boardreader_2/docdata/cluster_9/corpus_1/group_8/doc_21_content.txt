Reno police officers are responding to a reported robbery at the Bank of America near the Grand Sierra Resort.  Officers are taking witness reports and gathering evidence at the bank, located at 2597 Mill St..  'All we know right now is that the bank was robbed by a black male,' Reno police sergeant Ernie Leyva said.  A witness at the scene reported seeing police officers waving customers away from the bank.  Stay with RGJ.com for more details as they become available.
