RENO, Nev. -- Reno police are looking for a bank robber who may have red dye on the stolen money.  A 5-foot-7 white man between 20 and 30 years old robbed the Bank of America branch at a Raley's store on South Virginia about 10:45 a.m. Tuesday. Police say he handed a teller a note that indicated he was armed and demanded money.  He fled the bank with an undisclosed amount of money that may have been in a sack with an exploding dye pack.
