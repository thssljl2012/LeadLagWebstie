Fredericksburg native Harvey Thomas is the Continental Basketball Association's player of the week.  Thomas averaged 21.6 points and 7.2 rebounds a game for the Albany (N.Y.) Patroons as they went 4-1 last week, all on the road. Included was a 32-point outburst in a win at Minot (S.D.).  Thomas, who played at Georgetown and Baylor universities, leads the CBA in scoring at 22.8 points a game and is averaging 8.2 rebounds, fifth in the league.
