HYDE PARK, OH (FOX19)-- Cincinnati Police are investigating a bank robbery that occurred Thursday afternoon at US Bank.  Around 4:45pm, a man enter the bank at the 3400 block of Edwards Road. The suspect then handed the teller a note demanding money. After the cash was taken he fled the scene in an unknown direction.   Investigators say the suspect is a man who stands six feet tall and weighs 160 to 180 pounds. He was wearing a green army field jacket, black pants, and a black knit cap.   Anyone with information about the robbery or the suspect is asked to call Crimestoppers at 513-352-3040.
