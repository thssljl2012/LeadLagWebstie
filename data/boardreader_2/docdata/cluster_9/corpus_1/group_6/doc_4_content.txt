Southfield Police are looking for a man they say robbed a Bank of America on Greenfield Road about 11:30 a.m. today.  According to police, the man -- using a note to imply that he had a bomb -- was given an undisclosed amount of money. Then he ran from the bank on foot.  No one was injured in the robbery. The man is described as being between ages 45 and 55, 5-foot-7 and about 180 pounds, police said. He was wearing a dark jacket, blue hooded sweatshirt and large sunglasses.  Anyone with information is asked to call the Southfield Police Department at 248-796-5500.
