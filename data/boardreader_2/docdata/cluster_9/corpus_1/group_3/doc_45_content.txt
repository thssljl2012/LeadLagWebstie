Oct. 16--City robbery detectives have arrested one man and are searching for a second in connection with a bank robbery last week in Bloomfield.Dwayne Carey, 44, of Wilkinsburg was arrested Wednesday by city robbery detectives and Wilkinsburg police and charged with robbery, making terroristic threats and conspiracy for the holdup that occurred Oct. 9 at the First Commonwealth Bank in the 4900 block of Liberty Avenue.Police are still searching for a man identified as Kenneth Mosley, no age or address given. Anyone with information about Mosley is asked to call detectives at 412-323-7151.
