Police in Bradford are appealing for information following a robbery in Lister Park, Manningham. The male victim was walking through the park in the direction of Manningham Lane when he was attacked by three men.   He had just gone past the tennis courts when he heard a noise. When he turned around he was punched by one of the men, who demanded his mobile phone. Cash was taken from the victim and the three suspects made off into the park.   The victim was taken to BRI for treatment to facial injuries.   It is believed the suspects were all in their late teens and two of them spoke with Bradford accents. All three are thought to be Asian in appearance. One of the suspects was wearing a white top.   Anyone with any information is asked to contact Bradford South CID on 0845 60 60 606.
