ROYAL PALM BEACH A bank robber was caught in a city neighborhood after a brief manhunt this afternoon, authorities said.  About 1:15 p.m., the robber, who wasn't named but was described as 40 to 50 years old, walked into Regions Bank at 1184  Blvd., demanded money from the teller, fled on foot and then got into a car, according to a  Sheriff's Office bulletin circulated this afternoon.
