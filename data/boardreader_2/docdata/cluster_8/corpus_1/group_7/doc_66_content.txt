Preview. Bernie Madoff bilks millions out of gullible investors, Citibank opts to buy a pricey private jet after receiving billions in bailout funds, and AIG officials escape to a sun-dappled luxury resort days after a bailout of their own. The rich always seem to be up to no good, and in his new novel, "The Millionaires," ($24.95, W.W. Norton) Inman Majors adds the name of the Cole brothers to the pantheon of the rich and reckless.
