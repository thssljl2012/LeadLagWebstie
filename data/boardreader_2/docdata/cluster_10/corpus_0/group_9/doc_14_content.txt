Former Treasury Secretary Henry M. Paulson and Federal Reserve Chairman Ben S. Bernanke threatened to oust Bank of America Corp.'s board and its chief executive, Kenneth Lewis, if the bank called off its purchase of Merrill Lynch &amp; Co., Lewis said in sworn testimony released Thursday.
