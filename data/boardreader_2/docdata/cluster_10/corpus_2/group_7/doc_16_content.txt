 FNM Message Board Posts          Recs: 1              Bank Of America Will Pay Some Bonuses Over 18                    Bank of America Corp. (BAC) reversed course on a controversial decision to stagger 2008 bonus payments over a four-year period, and instead will pay some bankers their money over an 18-month period.               The Charlotte-based bank confirmed on Thursday it is "shortening the vesting schedule for a portion" of the awards. But the amount of money being given to employees hasn't increased.               There was a backlash inside BofA after employees in the investment banking and capital markets group were told their bonuses wouldn't be paid all at once because of financial troubles at the bank. The backlash intensified after Merrill Lynch &amp; Co. doled out its bonuses all at once before the broker was acquired by Bank of America.
