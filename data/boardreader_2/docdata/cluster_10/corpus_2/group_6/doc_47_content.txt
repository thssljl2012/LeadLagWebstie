       14:45  BAC Bank of America: CNBC says Fed, FDIC &amp; Treasury officials are discussing a Citi-type guarantee for BAC of between $100-200 bln (8.70 -1.50) -Update-     CNBC commentator says the Fed, FDIC and Treasury are working on guarantee for BAC with regard to Merrill, totalling $100-$200 bln.
