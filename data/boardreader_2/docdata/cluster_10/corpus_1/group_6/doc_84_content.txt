CHARLOTTE -- Bank of America has released its latest earnings report, as well as details on a deal with the federal government to get $20 billion more from the emergency bailout fund than theyve already received.  The Charlotte-based bank says in the last quarter of 2008 the company suffered a net loss of $1.79 billion. Thats down from a net income of $215 million during the same period in 2007.  In addition, the bank has announced a deal with the federal government to get $20 billion from the bailout fund. The deal also guarantees against losses of up to $118 billion in troubled assets.  Bank of America will use the money to help absorb losses from its acquisition of Merrill Lynch.
