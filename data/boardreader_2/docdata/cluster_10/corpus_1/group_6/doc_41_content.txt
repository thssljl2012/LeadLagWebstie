(RTTNews) - Following news of a lawsuit that centers on alleged violation of the federal Fair Labor Standards Act, shares of Bank of America (BAC   Quote   Chart   News   PowerRating) have dropped $0.76 to $10.67.  The accusation comes as the company supposedly failed to pay overtime to back office employees who facilitate transactions of the company's derivatives products.  With the decline, the stock has fallen to a level that, if closed at, would mark its lowest closing level in over twelve years.
