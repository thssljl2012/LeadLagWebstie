Bank of America Corp. will cut 30,000 to 35,000 jobs over the next three years at part of its acquisition of Merrill Lynch &amp;amp; Co. Inc. Charlotte-based BofA (NYSE:BAC) says it will determine a final number early next year. Bank of America employs 1,800 in the Piedmont Triad.   The reductions are coming from both companies and affect all lines of business and staff units.
