(Xinhua via COMTEX News Network) -- China's FTSE Xinhua Index Limited (FXI) announced recently that its Xinhua Lehman China Aggregate Index co-founded with the collapsed U.S. investment bank giant Lehman Brothers has been renamed as Xinhua Barclays Capital Aggregate Index.   According to FXI, the counting measures and management of the index will remain unchanged.   Xinhua Lehman China Aggregate Index, which was launched on March 15, 2004, provides a comprehensive series of data released by China's stock exchanges of the T-bond, bank notes and corporate bonds issued through Chinese interbank market.
