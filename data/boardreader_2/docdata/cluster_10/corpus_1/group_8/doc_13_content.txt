CHARLOTTE  Bank of America CEO Ken Lewis says his request for money to support the Merrill Lynch acquisition was a 'tactical mistake.'  Lewis made that comment to The Financial Times Monday.  Bank of America received an additional $20 billion in federal funding when they acquired the struggling company in December.  Lewis also said he would serve as Bank of America's CEO until all of the $45 billion in funding is repaid to the government.  Bank of America's stock, and others, plunged on Wall Street Monday.
