 BRITAIN'S most expensive player visits the Riverside on Wednesday - and there's still time to book your seat for the game.Freescoring Brazilian Robinho and his compatriot Afonso Alves will be hoping to get one over each other, with vital Barclays Premier League points at stake. Tickets are on general sale and are available , by calling 0844 499 1234 or from the Riverside ticket office.
