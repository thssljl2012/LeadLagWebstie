 The British government has announced an $87.5 billion plan to partially nationalize its major banks in a bid to restore economic stability.The Treasury made the announcement Wednesday before markets opened, saying eight banks have signed up for the so-called recapitalization plan. The plan offers up to $87.5 billion in the form of preference shares.The Treasury said the eight banks are Abbey, Barclays, HBOS, HSBC, Lloyds TSB, Nationwide Building Society, Royal Bank of Scotland and Standard Chartered. @Nyx.CommentBody@
