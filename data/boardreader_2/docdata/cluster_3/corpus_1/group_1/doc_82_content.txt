 Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd (ANZ) , the nation's third-largest lender, is considering increasing two Australian dollar bond issues, a fund manager has said. The bank may increase a note maturing in September 2010 at 65 basis points over swap or BBSW and/or a note due in 2011 at 90 basis points. Details such as volume and timing were not available, the fund manager said. ANZ is rated AA by S&amp;amp;P and Aa1 by Moody's.
