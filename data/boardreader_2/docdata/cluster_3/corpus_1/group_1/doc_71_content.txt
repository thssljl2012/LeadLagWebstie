 SYDNEY, Aug 22 (Reuters) - Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd , Australia's third-biggest lender, said a review of its securities lending business had revealed management weakness. It said ANZ did not adequately identify and manage risks arising from securities lending business. ANZ undertook the preview following the collapse of Opes Prime Group, to which the bank was a lender. ($1=A$1.14) (Reporting by Denny Thomas).ngCOPYRIGHTCopyright Thomson Financial News Limited 2008.
