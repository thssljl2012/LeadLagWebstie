 Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd (ANZ) , Australia's third-largest lender, said it has added a total of $1.17 billion to its bonds due in 2010 and 2011. The three-tranche offer included $120 million of fixed rate notes due in Sept. 2010 at 63 basis points over swap, $700 million of floating rate notes due in Nov. 2011 at 90 bps over BBSW and $350 million of fixed rate notes due in Nov. 2011 at 90 bps over swap.
