CHARLOTTE -- Bank of America plans to cut 139 jobs at its Ballantyne Corporate Place office. The layoffs in the preferred services unit will take effect March 10.  The company says the cuts are a result of an overlap in positions due to the merger with Merrill Lynch.  Bank of America has said it plans to cut 30,000to 35,000 jobs across the combined company during the next three years. These cuts are part of that number.
