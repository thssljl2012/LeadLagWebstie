Equity Capital MarketsThe equity capital markets suffered the slowest week so far this year, with only $25 million raised from four issues in the Asia ex-Japan region. The league table rankings remain unchanged with Citi sitting at the top on $4.7 billion from 19 issues, followed by UBS with $3.9 billion and Credit Suisse on $3.7 billion.Debt Capital MarketsIt was another silent week in the debt capital markets with zero trades for the fourth week running. HSBC leads the field in the league table ranking with $2.6 billion, closely followed by Barclays Capital on $2.5 billion and Deutsche Bank with $2.2 billion.  A dismal week in the equity and debt capital markets.
