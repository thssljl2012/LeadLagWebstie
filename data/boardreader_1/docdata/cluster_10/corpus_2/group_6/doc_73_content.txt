Wed 21 jan     Financial shares advanced as investors hunted for bargains in the hard-hit sector. JPMorgan Chase &amp; Co soared 25 percent and Bank of America Corp gained almost 31 percent on news that Chief Executive Kenneth Lewis spent almost $1.2 million to buy 200,000 shares on Tuesday.    "After the news that Bank of America insiders have bought stock, there has been some aggressive buying in both the stock and call options on hopes that the shares have hit bottom," said William Lefkowitz, options strategist at brokerage firm vFinance Investments in New York.
