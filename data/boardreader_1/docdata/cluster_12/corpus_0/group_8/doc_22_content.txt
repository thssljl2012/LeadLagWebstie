U.S. BankCategory:  Banking ServicesU.S. Bank is a financial services company operated by its parent company, U.S. Bancorp. It provides a wide array of banking services and products. The U.S. Bank website is designed to provide consumers easy and secure access to their bank accounts through the Web or using their mobile devices. U.S. Bank is the sixth largest bank in the United States with about $247 billion in assets and was number 122 in the Fortune 500 list of major U.S. companies in 2008.http://www.usbank.com/
