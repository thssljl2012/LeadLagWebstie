British High Street Bank Lloyds TSB have announced the next generation in Mobile Banking here in the UK with the first Mobile Banking application for mobiles that allows a range of banking services.  The java application will allow customers to securely move money between their Lloyds TSB accounts, view account balances and set-up a range of text alerts all for 2.50 a month. See the Lloyds TSB Mobile Services site for more information.
