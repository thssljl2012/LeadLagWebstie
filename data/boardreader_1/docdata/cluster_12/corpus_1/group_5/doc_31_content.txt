The following financial services industry appointments were announced on Monday. To inform us of other job changes, please e-mail moves@thomsonreuters.com.   RBC CAPITAL MARKETS   RBC Capital Markets, the corporate and investment banking arm of Royal Bank of Canada, said it has appointed David Hann as director, equity sales Asia. Hann will be based in the company's Hong Kong office.   PRINCIPLE CAPITAL HOLDINGS S.A.   Principle Capital, the independent investment management and advisory group, said it has appointed Mark Whitfeld as Managing Director. The appointment is with effect from January 1, 2009.   SK CAPITAL PARTNERS   SK Capital Partners said it has appointed Clark Winter as the firm's chief investment officer. Prior to joining SK Capital, Winter was a Managing Director and Director of Portfolio Strategy for Goldman Sachs.
