Feb 9 (Reuters) - McKesson Corp, (News) the largest U.S.   pharmaceuticals distributor, on Monday sold $700 million of   notes in two-part debt sale, said IFR, a Thomson Reuters   service.   The size of the deal was increased from an originally   planned $500 million.   Bank of America and JP Morgan were the joint bookrunning   managers for the sale.
