SYDNEY, Feb 18 (Reuters) - Bank of New Zealand (BNZ), a unit of National Australia Bank , has sold NZ$180 million ($91 million) of five-year bonds backed by the New Zealand government at 80 basis points over swap, the bank said on Wednesday.  The fixed rate offer is the first issue backed by the government under its wholesale guarantee programme put in place last year to help firms weather the global credit crisis.  BNZ solely led the offer. ($1=1.972 New Zealand Dollar) (Reporting by Cecile Lefort)
