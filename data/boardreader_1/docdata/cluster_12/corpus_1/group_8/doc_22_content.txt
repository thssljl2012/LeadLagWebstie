Jenny Fagg has been appointed as the new chief executive of ANZ National Bank, a move that will bring continuity to the company during a tough time for banks.  She takes over the position from Graham Hodges, who has been appointed deputy chief executive of the wider ANZ Group.  Dr Fagg has been promoted from her current position as deputy chief executive of ANZ National, and is also currently managing director retail distribution for the company.  Upon taking up her appointment she will also join the ANZ Groups management board.  ANZ National chairman Sir Dryden Spring says, Jenny Fagg is familiar with the New Zealand business and is recognised as one of ANZ Groups most talented executives with a strong background in retail banking in Australia and New Zealand and in risk management.  She will take up her new role on May 1.
