SYDNEY, March 12 (Reuters) - Bank of New Zealand (BNZ), a unit of National Australia Bank, has increased its 2014 government-backed bond to NZ$285 million ($185 million), it said on Thursday. Deal details are as follows: Issuer: Bank of New Zealand Guarantor: The government of New Zealand Facility: Fixed rate registered transferable deposits increase Law: New Zealand Amount issued: NZ$105 million Total issued: NZ$285 million Maturity: Feb. 20, 2014 Set date: March 11 Coupon: 4.775% Yield: 4.985% Spread: +80bp over swap or +112bp over 2013 NZGB Issue price 99.086447 + 0.250621 (accrued) Lead(s): BNZ Issuer rating: AAA (S&amp;P), Aaa (Moody's) ($1=1.541 Australian Dollar) (Reporting by Cecile Lefort) Keywords: BNZ/BONDS (cecile.lefort@reuters.com; +612-9373-1234; Reuters Messaging: cecile.lefort.reuters.com@reuters.net) COPYRIGHT Copyright Thomson Reuters 2009.
