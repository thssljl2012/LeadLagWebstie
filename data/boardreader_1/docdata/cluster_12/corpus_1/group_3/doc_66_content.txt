 The financial services sector in Liverpool is starting to regain some of its previous strength after years of decline. In recent years a number of financial organisations, including Lloyds TSB and Panmure Gordon, have set up operations in Liverpool and there are now estimated to be 200,000 jobs in professional services across Merseyside.
