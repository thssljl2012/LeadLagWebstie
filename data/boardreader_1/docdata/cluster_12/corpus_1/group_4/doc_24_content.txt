Foster's Group Ltd says global brewer Molson Coors Brewing Company has bought a substantial stake in the company.  Foster's said the principal party behind a substantial shareholding in the company reported by Deutsche Bank on September 25 is Colorado-based Molson Coors.  'Foster's has not been informed of the precise extent of Molson Coors' interest in the company nor their intentions with respect to that interest,' it said in a statement.  On September 25, Deutsche Bank told the stock exchange it had accumulated a 5.26 per cent interest in Foster's.  Molson Coors has operation in the US, Canada and the UK.
