SYDNEY, Nov 7 (Reuters) - Commonwealth Bank of Australia Ltd  (CBA) has A$240 million ($160 million) senior debt exposure to child-care group ABC Learning Centres Ltd , which collapsed earlier this week, the bank said in a statement on Friday.  CBA also holds 4.456 million hybrid notes in ABC which have a carrying value of about A$220 million, following a A$100 million post tax write down of these notes last month. ($1=A$1.50) (Reporting by Denny Thomas)
