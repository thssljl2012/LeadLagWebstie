SACRAMENTO, Calif. (KCBS) -- State Insurance Commissioner Steve Poizner reassured California policy holders about the crisis affectign American International Group or AIG. Poizner who is running for governor says Golden State AIG customers need not worry. 'The $85 billion loan to AIG does not put a lien on any of the assets of AIG's insurance companies (in California),' said Poizner. AIG owns 25 insurance companies in the state, covering everything from auto insurance to homeowner's insurance to worker's compensation.  Poizner says monitoring the AIG situation is his number one priority.(clo)
