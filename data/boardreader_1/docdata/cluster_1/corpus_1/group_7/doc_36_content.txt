Money &amp; Banking- Life Insurance  Mumbai, Feb. 19  Tata AIG Life Insurance has launched a unit linked product called InvestAssure Apex. The plan would enable the policy holders to enjoy returns based on the highest net asset value (NAV) declared over 100 months. The policy holder will be guaranteed the highest NAV among the 100 NAVs if the policy remains in force till maturity, said a press release issued by the bank. -Our Bureau  More Stories on : Life Insurance  Article   E-Mail :: Comment :: Syndication ::  Printer Friendly Page
