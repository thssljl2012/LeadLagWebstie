NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--American International Group, Inc. (AIG) has announced that Christina Pretto has joined AIG as Vice President, Corporate Media Relations. Ms. Pretto will oversee all aspects of AIGs corporate media relations, including developing and implementing AIGs media relations strategy, counseling senior management on media affairs, and coordinating media activities with AIGs communications professionals around the world.
