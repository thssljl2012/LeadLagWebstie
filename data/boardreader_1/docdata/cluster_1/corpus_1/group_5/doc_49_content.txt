NEW YORK, Dec 9 (Reuters) - The following financial services industry appointments were announced on Tuesday. To inform us of other job changes, please send an e-mail to moves@thomsonreuters.com. AMERICAN INTERNATIONAL GROUP INC The giant insurer, which was bailed out by the U.S. government earlier this year, named Peter Eastwood as chief executive of a unit of its AIG commercial insurance division, Lexington Insurance Company. Eastwood, who joined AIG in 1991, assumes the position from Kevin H.  Kelley, who has left the company to join Ironshore, a Bermuda-based property-casualty insurer that largely operates out of the Lloyd's of London insurance market. DELOITTE FINANCIAL ADVISORY SERVICES LLP The financial advisory subsidiary of accounting firm Deloitte LLP named Irene Walsh a managing director and U.S. leader of its infrastructure and project finance advisory practice and James Ziglar Jr., as senior vice president. Both Walsh and Ziglar formerly worked for UBS AG and will be based in New York. UBS AG The Swiss bank's global asset management division named John Dugenske as head of its fixed income business for the United States and Canada. He takes up his duties next month, and will be based in Chicago. Dugenske joins UBS Global Asset Management from Neuberger Berman, the former asset management business of Lehman Brothers, which he joined in 2004.
