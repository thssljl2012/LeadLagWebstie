Fortune Brands, Inc. today announced that the company's September 17th presentation at the Bank of America Investment Conference will be available live over the Internet.  The presentation by Bruce Carbonari, president and chief executive officer of Fortune Brands, will begin at approximately 11:30 a.m. ET on Wednesday, September 17, 2008. To access the webcast, go to "Webcasts" in the Investor Relations section of the Fortune Brands web site, www.fortunebrands.com. It is recommended that listeners log on 10 minutes prior to the start of the presentation.
