 A NEW sports facility is being launched this month with an indoor football tournament.The recently-built multi-use games area in London Road, Clacton, is being launched on July 22, with an opening event organised by crime reduction charity Nacro.It will include a futsal tournament for young people - a form of indoor football.Barclays Spaces for Sports provided the project with 50,000, in a partnership with Groundwork, Nacro and the Football Foundation.
