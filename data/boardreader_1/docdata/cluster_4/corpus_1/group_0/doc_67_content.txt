 KENNER -- A drive-through customer at a Kenner bank helped police nab two robbery suspects.Police say it happened Wednesday morning when the customer witnessed the robbery and dialed 911. The driver followed a sport utility vehicle driving away from the scene and gave police updates until the SUV crashed into a taxi. Police sealed off the surrounding neighborhood and soon had the suspects in custody.There were no injuries.Lieutenant Wayne McInnis of the police department says the customer at the drive-through window called 911 after he saw a teller raise her hands as a man pointed a gun at her.
