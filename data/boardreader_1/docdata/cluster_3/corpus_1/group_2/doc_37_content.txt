First Minister Alex Salmond has called off a visit to the United States as one of the country's big financial institutions announced it was in 'advanced' merger talks.Mr Salmond called off a planned four-day visit to the Ryder Cup golf tournament in order to press the Scottish interest in talks with Halifax Bank of Scotland and Lloyds TSB,HBOS, whose share price has plunged in recent days, said it was in 'advanced' talks on a merger that would create a 30 billion banking giant.
