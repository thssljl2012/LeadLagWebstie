 Bolton-based Loanmakers (AIM: LMH) admitted today it had suffered a new blow after First Plus, its main supplier, said it would stop accepting new business from August 9.The embattled company, formerly known as Debtmatters, said the move would adversely affect its business.First Plus is Barclays main secured loan brand and the largest provider of secured loans to brokers such as Loanmakers.In a stock exchange statement, Loanmakers said it would be undertaking an immediate review of the business to see how it can alleviate the impact of todays announcement. This will encompass sourcing additional capacity from other providers, as well as a review of the companys current cost structure.
