Countrywide Chairman and CEO Angelo Mozilo might just be in some very big trouble: SEC Begins Formal Probe Of Countrywide The Securities and Exchange Commission escalated its scrutiny of Countrywide Financial Corp. into a formal investigation, according to a regulatory filing by Bank of America Corp.
