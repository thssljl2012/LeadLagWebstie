Global Stock Markets Collapse; World Action Urged On Crisis: LONDON (AFP) - Global stock markets spiralled ever deeper into the vortex on Friday as pressure mounted for world leaders to throw up a decisive wall to contain the worst financial inferno since the Great Depression.Stock exchanges from Tokyo to London suffered more staggering losses  adding to the turmoil for finance ministers from the Group of Seven richest nations to discuss in Washington. We are drowning in a sea of red numbers, said Barclays Wealth analyst Henk Potts.
