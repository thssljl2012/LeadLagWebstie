Bangor&amp;#8217;s annual Festival of Lights parade is this Saturday, December 6, at 4:30 pm. The parade begins on Railroad Street, turns right onto Main Street, crosses Union Street, proceeds along Main Street through downtown, crosses Hammond and State streets onto Central Street, turns right on Harlow, and crosses State Street to Exchange Street, ending by Bank of America.
