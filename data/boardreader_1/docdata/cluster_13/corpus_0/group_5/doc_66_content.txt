Citibank Korea(CEO: YK Ha, www.citibank.co.kr) hosted an idea competition from Nov 17 through Dec 14 for college students on the internet banking and marketing strategy and announced the winners on Dec 23.  The competition proceeded with active participation of 348 teams from across the country. The 1st place award went to Dongkook University team that suggested Internet acquisition plan according to life cycle, and 2 runner-ups and 10 3rd place winners were also announced.
