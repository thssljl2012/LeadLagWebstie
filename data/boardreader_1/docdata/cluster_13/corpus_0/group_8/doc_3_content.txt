A bomb has exploded in a suburb of the Greek capital Athens. The explosion caused considerable damage, but there were no casualties. A branch of Citibank was the target of the bomb attack.Last month a heavy bomb was discovered outside the bank's headquarters in the Greek capital. It was defused before it went off, but had it exploded it could have caused the building to collapse.The police believe an anarchist group is behind the bombing campaign. In 2007, the Revolutionary Struggle used rockets to attack the US embassy in Athens.
