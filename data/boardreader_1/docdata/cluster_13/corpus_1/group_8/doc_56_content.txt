Worcester Business Journal Staff Writer  Today  UniBank of Whitinsville has hired Deborah L. Larsen as senior vice president of commercial banking.  Larsen previously served as senior vice president for Bank of America's Central Massachusetts middle market division.  She has 25 years of commercial banking experience, including time at Bank of America predecessors First National Bank of Boston, BankBoston and FleetBoston Financial.  In her new position, Larsen is responsible for developing new commercial banking business and managing the bank's new Worcester loan office.  Larsen has an MBA and a bachelor's in business administration from Assumption College. She lives in Sutton with her husband Bruce and daughter Jackie.
