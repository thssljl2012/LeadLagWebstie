Canyon County Habitat for Humanity and Bank of America will dedicate a new home at 3:30 p.m. Monday at 518 19th Ave. N., Nampa.  This home is part of the bank's $6 million commitment to Habitat for Humanity International.  The Olvera family, who gave more than 250 hours of sweat equity toward building the home, will take possession of it by the end of the year.  For information about supporting or volunteering with Canyon County Habitat for Humanity, call 208-453-4968 or visit www.2CHabitat.org.
