WASHINGTON-Montgomery County Police are investigating a bank robbery in Germantown Saturday.   The robbery happened around 9:30 this morning at the Bank of America branch at 19707 Germantown Road.   Police say a man entered the bank and passed a note to the teller demanding money.   The bank employee gave the man an undisclosed sum, and the suspect left the building.   No one was hurt in the incident.   The suspect is described as a white male in his 40s. He stands 5'6' tall, and weighs 160 pounds. He wore a gray/green long-sleeve plaid shirt, blue jeans, and a light-colored baseball cap.   If you have any information about the suspect, call Major Crimes Division Robbery Section at 240-773-5100. Callers may remain anonymous.   Police say today's incident is not related to other bank robberies that have happened in the area in recent weeks. (
