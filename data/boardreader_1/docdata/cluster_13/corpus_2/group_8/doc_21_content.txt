Bank of America Buys Land for Future Data Center Staley Farms Parcel Sells for $2 Million By Shavon Shockley March 4, 2009 Bank of America acquired 26.2 acres at the southeast corner of Highway 291 and North Woodland Avenue in Kansas City, MO, from Extell Development Co. for $2 million, or about $76,335 per acre. The land is part of Staley Farms area and will serve as a data center for Bank of America. John Stafford of Heise-Meyer Properties represented the seller. Brian Oley of Jones Lang LaSalle represented the buyer. Please refer to CoStar COMPS #1649327 for additional information on this transaction.
