 Bill Richardson was born in Pasadena, California.   His father, William Blaine Richardson Jr. (died in 1972), was an American Citibank executive   who grew up in Boston, Massachusetts  and lived and worked in Mexico City.  His mother, Mara Luisa Lpez-Collada Mrquez  is Mexican    and was his father's secretary  and a socialite.  Richardson's father was born in Nicaragua.  Just before Richardson was born, his father sent his mother to California to give birth because, as Richardson explained, "My father had a complex about not having been born in the United States."  Richardson, a U.S. citizen by birthright, was raised during his childhood in Mexico City.
