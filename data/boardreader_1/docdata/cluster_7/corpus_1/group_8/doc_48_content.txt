The Australian bond market has opened weaker, moving in line with longer-dated US Treasuries during weekend trading and on increasing speculation the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) will leave interest rates on hold in March.  At 0830 AEDT, the yield on the Commonwealth Government March 2019 bond was 4.428 per cent, up from Friday's close of 4.403 per cent, while the yield on the April 2012 bond was at 3.358 per cent, up from 3.240 per cent.
