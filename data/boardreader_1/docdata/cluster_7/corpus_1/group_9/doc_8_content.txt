The federal government will reportedly lend support to small businesses in the May budget, as corporate investment dwindles. Federal Treasurer Wayne Swan is also investigating the effects of the global credit crisis on small and medium size enterprises in preparation for a broad policy response to help protect the sector's 3.75 million jobs, according to the The move comes as the government and Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) warn it is virtually impossible for Australia to avoid recession. Growth in developed countries is projected to shrink by 4.3 per cent over the same time, a contraction which Mr Swan says has significant implications for Australia.
