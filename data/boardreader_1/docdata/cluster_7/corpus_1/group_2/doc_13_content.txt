Bank of Queensland Ltd has reaffirmed its earnings guidance of 10% EPS growth.Managing director David Liddy also confirmed the banks recent success in raising $309.5 million through a 2.5 year syndicated loan which had wide support from Asian and European institutions.The bank has no exposure to Lehman Bros Holding International, which filed for chapter 11 bankruptcy in the US on Monday. The bank also has no exposure to Merrill Lynch or American International Group Inc, but AIG Australia is an underwriter of a number of insurance policies held by the bank for its protection in relation to dishonesty and fraud, professional indemnity and directors and officers liability. Bank of Queensland also noted it has no exposure to any troubled Gold Coast based property development groups.
