 SYDNEY, Sept 16 (Reuters) - Australia and New Zealand Banking Group (ANZ.AX: Quote, Profile, Research, Stock Buzz) said on Tuesday its total exposure to troubled U.S bank Lehman Brothers (LEH.N: Quote, Profile, Research, Stock Buzz) was about $120 million.ANZ, Australia's third largest bank, said it was not in a position to provide an estimate of likely losses.(Reporting by Mette Fraende, Editing by Mark Bendeich)
