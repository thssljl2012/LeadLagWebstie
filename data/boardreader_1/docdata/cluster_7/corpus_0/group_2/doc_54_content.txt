SAN FRANCISCO, CA--(Marketwire - September 26, 2008) -  To help investors navigate the recent turbulence in the markets, iShares is posting videos of insights and commentary by iShares and Barclays Global Investors' (BGI) experts. Lee Kranefuss, Global CEO of iShares, along with Russ Koesterich, BGI's Head of Investment Strategy, and Pete Wilson, BGI's Head of Fixed Income Strategy, are among those experts sharing their ideas about what has caused the current economic situation, what it means for the markets for the long term, and how iShares exchange traded funds can help investors adapt to a changing investing world.
