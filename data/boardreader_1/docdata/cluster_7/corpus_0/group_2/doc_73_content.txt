Westpac Banking will acquire St. George Bank for A$18 mln (WSJ). Westpac made its initial offer in May (prior post). While the original offer of 1.31 shares of Westpac for each share of St. George remains unchanged, Westpac increased the size of a special dividend payment to St. George shareholders by A$.28 to A$1.25 per share. St. George shareholders will vote on the deal on November 13. The deal will create Australia's largest bank by market capitalization.
