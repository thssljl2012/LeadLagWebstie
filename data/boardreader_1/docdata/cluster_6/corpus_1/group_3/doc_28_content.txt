Fulham striker Andrew Johnson returns from suspension for tomorrows Barclays Premier League clash with Sunderland. Andranik Teymourian has been ruled for three to four months after the Iranian midfielder sustained a knee injury while playing for the reserves. Dickson Etuhu is unavailable due to a lack of match fitness but otherwise Fulham manager Roy Hodgson has a fully fit squad to select from. Provisional squad: Schwarzer, Pantsil, Hughes, Hangeland, Konchesky, Davies, Bullard, Murphy, Gera, Zamora, Johnson, Seol, Zuberbuhler, Nevland, Dempsey, Andreasen, Kallio, Baird, Stoor, Gray.
