      Australia vs England - Game 1                Warne wins first        Shane Warne has won his first game as England coach as his side cruised to a comfortable six-wicket win over Australia in the first game of the Commonwealth Bank Series today.    England reached the target with 4 overs to spare through an 81 by Marcus Trescothick and 49 by Kevin Pietersen as they both discovered some form.        Marcus Trescothick celebrates England's win.
