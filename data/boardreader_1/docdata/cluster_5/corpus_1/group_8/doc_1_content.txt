Girish (CBA) bt Sameer Shroff (TNBSA) 3-2 (97-27, 65-29, 24-68, 40-64, 49-23); Damu Menon bt Sameer 3-0 (70-32, 56-14, 60-13); Girish bt Damu 3-0 (66-14, 74-15, 62-54); Girish bt Kamaraj (TNBSA) 3-1 (one frame awarded, 28-45, 61-30, 62-46); Prem Prakash (CBA) bt Sameer 3-0 (66-55, 56-10, 65-16).
