Melbourne, Feb 8 : Pace bowler Peter Siddle was included in the Australian squad today for the final two ODIs of the ongoing Commonwealth Bank Series against New Zealand.  However, Ryan Harris and David Warner have both been released from the Australia squad and will return to state duties, Cricket Australia said in a statement.  Australia today defeated New Zealand by 32 runs in the third match of the five-match ODI series at the Sydney Cricket Ground to keep the series alive.   The world champions are trailing by 2-1 to Black Caps.The squad: Ricky Ponting (C), Michael Clarke (vc) Michael Hussey, Nathan Bracken, Callum Ferguson, Brad Haddin, Ben Hilfenhaus, Jame Hopes, David Hussey, Mitchell Johnson, Shaun Tait, Cameron White, Aadm Voges, Peter Siddle. --- UNI
