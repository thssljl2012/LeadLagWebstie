The benefits as said by the bank include the Maxi Rate that offers up to 6.5%of interest per year to help customers grow their savings. They can also access the Maxi Access service where they can access the features of a Citibank debit card as well as cash rewards, point rewards, and partner discount offers.  Melina Sutikno, head of retail bank at Citibank Indonesia, said: 'Maxi Save allows customers to better manage daily and monthly transactions and also enjoy the convenience and rewards offered by a Citibank debit card. Many savings accounts are focused on the short-term. Maxi Save however is unique and with the 1 Bill solution and mutual fund option, it allows customers to manage and invest for the future.'
