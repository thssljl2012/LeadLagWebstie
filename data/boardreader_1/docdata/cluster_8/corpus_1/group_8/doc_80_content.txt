WASHINGTON, March 17 (Reuters) - U.S. Senator Christopher Dodd, chairman of the Senate Banking Committee, made the following comments about the bailed-out insurance company American International Group at a hearing on insurance regulation on Tuesday. * Dodd said he wants details from the Federal Reserve on what it is doing to address the controversial executive bonuses at AIG, even as it gets billions of dollars in taxpayer aid. * Dodd said AIG's issues should not be confused with broader insurance sector.
