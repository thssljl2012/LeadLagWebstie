A general threat against politicians made this morning by a disgruntled Bank of America customer (see "Scanner Traffic") is being investigated by the Kootenai County Sheriff's Department. KCSD spokeswoman Kim Edmundson told Huckleberries that deputies are looking for a man in his mid- to late 50s who told a teller he wanted to withdraw some money in order to buy a gun and "shoot a politician."
