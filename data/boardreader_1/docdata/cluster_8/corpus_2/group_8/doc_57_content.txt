                       women to become majority workforce in usa                                                Bread, bacon, clams - whatever you call it, women are the ones bringing it home these days.      According to The New York Times over the past year, as companies from Citibank to GM announced massive layoffs, over 82 percent of the people getting pink slips have been men. Soon, women are projected to become the majority of the American workforce.      On the one hand, more women being primary money-makers is an amazing opportunity to get over the outdated idea that supporting a family is a man's job. But on the other hand, relationships can suffer when power dynamics shift.
