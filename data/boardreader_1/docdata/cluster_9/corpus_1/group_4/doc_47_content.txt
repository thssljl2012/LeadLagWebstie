COLUMBUS, Ohio A man suspected of robbing a Grove City bank earlier this month has been taken into custody, according to a release issued by the FBI.  Police arrested Karl E. Smith on Friday afternoon, the release said.  Smith is accused of robbing the Fifth Third Bank, located on Hoover Road, on Nov. 13.  No other details were immediately released by authorities.
