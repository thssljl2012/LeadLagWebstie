College students will be taking a trip to a police station to learn about life working in the force.   The students at Bradford College will look at police vehicles and equipment and hear presentations on topics such as Neighbourhood Policing, discipline and codes of conduct, drugs, prisoner handling, searching and the recruitment process.   Some students will also get the chance to visit specialist departments in Wakefield.   They are all taking the Public Services course which aims to give them a realistic introduction to work in any of the public service, including the police .
