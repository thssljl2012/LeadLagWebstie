AUGUSTA, Ga. - NBC Augusta 26 News is learning more about the man charged with robbing the Bank of America on Washington Road.   Alex Rogers served 30 years for robbing another bank. He got out of jail in 2001.  Deputies arrested him at the Warren-McDuffie County line for the Bank of America robbery on Friday.  Investigators say he could be linked to other robberies in the Augusta-Aiken area.
