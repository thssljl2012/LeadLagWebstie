Country stars Keith Urban and Taylor Swift are bringing their 'Escape Together World Tour' to U.S. Bank Arena on June 5. Tickets for the 7:30 p.m. concert, $20-$82.50, go on sale 10 a.m. Saturday.  Swift, whose album 'Fearless' was the biggest-selling record of 2008 by a female artist, holds the Billboard all-genre record for most Top 20 debuts in a year.  Tickets will be available online at ticketmaster.com, all Ticketmaster outlets, the U.S. Bank Arena Box Office or by phone at 800-745-3000.  Kings of Leon will play Riverbend's just-renamed PNC Pavilion 7:30 p.m. May 13. Tickets for the show, which also includes the Walkmen, go on sale 10 a.m. Saturday. Reserved tickets, $46, will be available at all Ticketmaster outlets.  National City Pavilion at Riverbend opened in May 2008. With the recent purchase of National City by PNC, the venue also changes names.  Kings of Leon's newest album, Only By the Night, debuted in the top five on the Billboard Top 200 and No. 1 on iTunes.
