Next Saturday the Dancing With The Stars tour is coming to US Bank Arena and this Friday night local dancers auditioned to be in the show.  Several couples took part in the qualifying event at Arthur Murray Dance Studio in downtown Cincinnati.  Ten were selected to be part of next week's show.  They will perform and be judged by a professional dancer from the cast of Dancing With The Stars.  9News Anchor Brendan Keefe served as one of the judges at Friday night's qualifying event.
