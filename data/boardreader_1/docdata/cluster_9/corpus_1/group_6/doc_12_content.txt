Chesapeake Police are investigating a robbery at Bank of America located at 4301 Indian River Road. On Monday, January 12, they say a black male entered the bank around 9:35 a.m., produced a note and demanded money, while displaying a handgun in his waistband.  The suspect is described as a male between the ages of 20-25, 6'4' 150 lbs, wearing all black, sunglasses, and a toboggan-style hat. Police say he exited the bank after receiving the money and was seen heading towards McDonald Road. A red dye pack reportedly exploded, possibly on the suspect, as he was leaving.  Anyone with information regarding this offense is asked to call the Crime Line at 1-888-L-O-C-K-U-U-P.
