Ormond Beach police and the FBI are looking for the man who robbed the Bank of America branch at 299 N. Nova Road on Friday afternoon. According to police, the suspect entered the bank, went to a teller and handed her a note, demanding money and stating that he had a gun. The teller complied, and the suspect left the bank. He was last seen running toward a nearby wooded area. He's described by police as a white male, about 5-feet, 7-inches tall and 160 pounds. He was wearing a camo-style cap, light-colored, long-sleeve shirt, and light-colored cargo pants.
