POMPANO BEACH, Fla. -- A pretty smile might go unnoticed, but rotten teeth are hard to forget.  That's how one bank teller described a man who robbed a Pompano Beach bank Tuesday morning.  According to the Broward Sheriff's Office, the man entered the Bank of America on South Ocean Boulevard and handed the teller a note demanding money.  A surveillance photo shows the man stuffing the cash into his shirt before getting away in a brown or rust-colored Chrysler PT Cruiser.  The man was last seen wearing a Polo T-shirt and a black cap with the word 'Weitz' written on it.Anyone who recognizes the man is asked to call the BSO.
