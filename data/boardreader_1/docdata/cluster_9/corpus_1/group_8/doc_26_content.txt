The FBI and Shaler police are investigating the robbery of First Commonwealth Bank on Saxonburg Boulevard Saturday.   At about 10 a.m., a man entered the bank, passed a note to a teller and fled on foot with an undetermined amount of cash. No one was injured in the robbery.   The man is described as clean shaven and thin, between the ages of 20 and 30 and about six feet tall. He was wearing blue jeans a baseball cap and an oversized gray hooded sweatshirt.
