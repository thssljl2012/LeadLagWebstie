SHALER, Pa. -- Police are searching for a man Saturday morning who they said robbed a bank in Shaler.  Allegheny County emergency officials told WTAE Channel 4 Action News the 911 call was received at about 10 a.m. after a man robbed the First Commonwealth Bank in the 900 block of Saxonburg Boulevard.
