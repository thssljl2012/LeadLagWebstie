CONCORD Two men armed with handguns held up a Sunvalley Mall bank Monday afternoon before driving away.  The robbery occurred at 4:40 p.m. at a Bank of America branch in the 1000 block of Sunvalley Boulevard, police said. The two men walked inside, brandished handguns and one of them jumped over the counter before demanding cash.  After getting an amount that police had not determined as of Monday evening, the robbers ran outside and drove away in a maroon sport utility vehicle.
