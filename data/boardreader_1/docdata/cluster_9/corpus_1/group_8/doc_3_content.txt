The robbery happened at the First Commonwealth Bank on Saxonburg Boulevard around 10 a.m. According to authorities, the suspect gave a note to the teller, but no weapon was shown during the incident. Officials say the man was able to get away with an undetermined amount of money. No injuries were reported. The suspect is described as a white man, between 20- to 30-years-old, with a thin build, about six feet tall and was clean shaven. Police say he was wearing a big, gray sweatshirt with a hood, a baseball cap and jeans. The Shaler Township Police Department and the FBI are investigating. Stay with KDKA for the latest details.
