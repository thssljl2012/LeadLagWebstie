ORLANDO, Fla. -- Orlando police said they were looking for two men involved with a botched robbery attempt. Eyewitness News was told that three men tried to hold up a woman in a parking lot near the Bank of America on East Colonial Drive  Tuesday night.However, several holiday shoppers saw what was happening and surrounded the thieves. One of the witnesses to the crime pulled out his gun and shot one of the robbers in the leg.  Police said the other two robbers ran from the scene. The woman was not hurt during the incident. Officials said the man who shot the robber will not face any charges.
