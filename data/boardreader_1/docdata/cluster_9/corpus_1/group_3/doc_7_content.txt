Kaitaia Police have arrested a 32-year-old unemployed man for the robbery of the Kaitaia ANZ Bank on Thursday October 16.Neville James Junior King, aged 32 years, appeared in the Kaitaia District Court yesterday charged with robbery and cultivation of cannabis.He was remanded in custody to appear in the Kaikohe District Court on Tuesday November 4.Detective Sergeant Trevor Beatson said members of the Kaitaia CIB and Tactical Response Group executed a search warrant in Mangamuka early yesterday morning as part of their hunt for the green nose robber.Police dubbed the suspect the green nose robber following reports that he had a distinctive green marking on his nose, which appeared to be a tattoo or something similar.Mr Beatson said this brought a large response from the public about his potential identity, however solid forensic work at the scene led to his identification.In an unusual twist Police found that the robber did not have a permanent nose tattoo, and in fact had no markings at all on his face. Apparently he used it as a disguise.
