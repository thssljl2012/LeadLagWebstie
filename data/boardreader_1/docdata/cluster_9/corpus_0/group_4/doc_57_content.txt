The annual showcase for the best in British archaeology and a central event in the archaeological calendar, the British Archaeological Awards (BAA), took place at the British Museum this week.Established in 1976, the BAA has grown to encompass 14 commendations and awards, covering every aspect of British archaeology from important discoveries and community archaeology to books, TV programmes and kids archaeology.An important winner for the Council of British Archaeology (CBA) was its Best ICT Project award for the Community Archaeology Forum (CAF) website which was set up two years ago as a resource for anyone working in community archaeology.
