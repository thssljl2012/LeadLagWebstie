Here, this one&amp;#039;s a lot easier:      Easy Biscuits    SUBMITTED BY: Brenda PHOTO BY: CBA MOM       &amp;amp;quot;Three ingredient biscuits.&amp;amp;quot;       RECIPE RATING:      Read Reviews (60)    Review/Rate This Recipe      --------------------------------------------------------------------------------      PREP TIME  10 Min     COOK TIME  25 Min     READY IN  35 Min     Original recipe yield 1 dozen           INGREDIENTS (Nutrition)   2 1/4 cups self-rising flour    3/4 cup shortening    1 cup milk     Add to Recipe Box          DIRECTIONS   Combine and mix ingredients together. Pour out on floured waxed paper. Pat the dough out with your hands until dough is not sticky (add a little flour if necessary). Fold double. Cut biscuits with a biscuit cutter.    Bake on a cookie sheet at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 20-25 minutes.
