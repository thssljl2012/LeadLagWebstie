SYDNEY, Feb 18 (Reuters) - Shares in Westpac Banking Corp. , Australia's second-largest bank, fell 2.5 percent on Wednesday after the bank reported a 2 percent fall in cash profit, and a more than five times rise in bad-debt charges.
