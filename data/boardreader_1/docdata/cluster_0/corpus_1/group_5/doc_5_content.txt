Reuters Messaging: ryan.vlastelica.reuters.com@reuters.net 0709 ET 12Dec2008-Auto bailout fails in Senate, shares tank ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ The U.S. Senate failed to reach a compromise to provide aid to the ailing auto industry, making it unlikely that any congressional action would be taken this year. Shares of General Motors, a Dow component, tumbled 35 percent to $2.68 while shares of Ford Motor Co shed 14.1 percent to $2.49.
