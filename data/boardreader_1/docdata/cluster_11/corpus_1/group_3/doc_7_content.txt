Barclays Capital downgrades IBM (NYSE: IBM) from Overweight to Equal Weight and lowers its price target from $130 to $108. Barclays cited risks from an weakening economy and large exposure to financial services. The firm lowered Q4 EPS estimates from $3.19 to $2.88 and FY09 EPS from $9.68 to $9.20. International Business Machines Corporation (IBM) develops and manufactures information technologies, including computer systems, software, networking systems, storage devices, and microelectronics worldwide.
